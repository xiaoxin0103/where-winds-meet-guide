# 🧩 Puzzle Mystic Arts Guide

Five special abilities that unlock puzzles and exploration throughout the game. Unlock them ALL early.

---

## The 5 Puzzle Mystic Arts

| # | Mystic Art | Unlocks | Use |
|---|-----------|---------|-----|
| 1 | **Tai Chi** | After prologue | Push/pull objects, activate mechanisms |
| 2 | **Meridian Touch** | Chapter 1 quest | Reveal hidden energy lines, connect points |
| 3 | **Celestial Sieze** | Chapter 1 quest | Grab and move distant objects |
| 4 | **Cloud Steps** | Chapter 1 quest | Double jump + air dash — reach high places |
| 5 | **Contortion** | Side quest | Shrink/squeeze through small gaps |

---

## How to Unlock Each

### Tai Chi
- **When:** Automatically during prologue
- **Use:** Interact with glowing objects marked with the Tai Chi symbol
- **Puzzle types:** Push blocks, rotate statues, open sealed doors

### Meridian Touch
- **When:** Chapter 1 main quest ("The Realm Endures")
- **Use:** See invisible energy lines. Connect them in the correct order.
- **Puzzle types:** Light sequences, constellation puzzles, energy flow

### Celestial Sieze
- **When:** Chapter 1 main quest
- **Use:** Grab distant objects and move them. Works like telekinesis.
- **Puzzle types:** Fetch items across gaps, activate distant switches

### Cloud Steps
- **When:** Chapter 1 main quest
- **Use:** Jump → jump again in air → dash. Reach rooftops and cliffs.
- **Puzzle types:** Platforming, reaching high ledges, crossing gaps

### Contortion
- **When:** Side quest in Qinghe (look for NPC near narrow cave)
- **Use:** Shrink to fit through tiny openings
- **Puzzle types:** Hidden caves, secret rooms, shortcuts

---

## Pro Tip

> Unlock all 5 before exploring deeply. Many puzzles require specific arts — if you skip one, you'll hit dead ends and have to backtrack.
