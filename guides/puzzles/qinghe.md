## Notable Qinghe Puzzles

### Harvestfall Village: Push Block Tutorial
- Your first puzzle. Push the block onto the glowing pressure plate.
- Teaches you Tai Chi interaction.

### Moonveil Mountain: Constellation
- Find the star pattern painted on the cliff wall.
- Use Meridian Touch to trace it in the sky.
- Reward: Torn Inner Way page.

### Sundara Land: Hidden Cave
- Look for a narrow crack in the cliff face north of Halo Peak.
- Requires Contortion to enter.
- Contains a chest with upgrade materials.

### Starfall Spring: Energy Flow
- Redirect energy beams to power 3 nodes.
- The correct order is shown by statues in the center.
- Unlocks the Midnight Mercy Sanctum entrance.

---

> **Tip:** Clear Qinghe puzzles before moving to Kaifeng. The rewards (Inner Way pages, upgrade mats) make the next region significantly easier.
