## Notable Kaifeng Puzzles

### Gracetown: Moon & Branch Alignment
- One of the trickiest alignment puzzles.
- Look at the branch's shadow on the ground. Align the moon object to match.
- Time of day matters — come at night for best visibility.

### Well of Heaven Camp: Stealth Infiltration
- Not a traditional puzzle, but requires puzzle-solving logic.
- Use Touch of Death to quietly eliminate guards.
- Reach the elevated sanctum without alerting the camp.
- 9 optional chests along the infiltration path.

### Palace of Annals: Light Sequence
- Activate energy points in the correct order.
- The sequence is shown on scrolls inside the palace.
- Reward: Strategic Sword Inner Way page.

### Mistveil Forest: Energy Maze
- Navigate a maze of invisible walls using Meridian Touch.
- Energy lines show the safe path.
- Leads to Soulshade Umbrella sanctum.

### Kaifeng City: Rooftop Platforming
- Chain Cloud Steps double jumps across rooftops.
- Collect glowing orbs along the way.
- Reach the final rooftop for a rare cosmetic piece.

---

> **Tip:** Kaifeng puzzles are significantly harder than Qinghe. Make sure all 5 Mystic Arts are unlocked and upgraded before tackling the harder ones.
