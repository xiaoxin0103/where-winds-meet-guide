# 🧩 Puzzle Types & Solutions

Every puzzle mechanic in Where Winds Meet and how to solve each one.

---

## Puzzle Type Reference

| Type | Mystic Art Needed | How to Solve |
|------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Push Block** | Tai Chi | Push blocks onto pressure plates or into matching slots |
| **Rotate Statue** | Tai Chi | Rotate statues to face the correct direction (hint: look for environmental clues) |
| **Light Sequence** | Meridian Touch | Connect energy points in the order shown by environmental hints |
| **Constellation** | Meridian Touch | Trace the pattern of stars shown on nearby walls |
| **Energy Flow** | Meridian Touch | Redirect energy streams to power devices |
| **Grabbing Puzzle** | Celestial Sieze | Pull distant objects to you, or place objects on distant platforms |
| **Pressure Plate** | None / Any | Stand on plates or place objects on them. Multiple players/objects often needed. |
| **Platforming** | Cloud Steps | Double jump + air dash to reach high platforms in sequence |
| **Hidden Passage** | Contortion | Find small cracks in walls and squeeze through |
| **Moon & Branch Alignment** | Tai Chi + Observation | Align objects to match moon/branch positions (look up!) |

---

## General Puzzle Tips

1. **Use Photo Mode** to scout the area and spot clues you'd miss in normal view.
2. **Look up** — many clues are on ceilings, walls, or in the sky.
3. **Listen** — some puzzles have audio cues (chimes, water, wind).
4. **Check your Mystic Art wheel** — if a puzzle doesn't respond, try a different art.
5. **Co-op helps** — some pressure plates need two players.

---

## The "Stuck" Checklist

If you're stuck on a puzzle, check:

- [ ] Do I have all 5 Mystic Arts unlocked?
- [ ] Have I looked at the environment for clues (symbols, colors, directions)?
- [ ] Is there a nearby NPC giving hints?
- [ ] Can Photo Mode reveal something I missed?
- [ ] Is this puzzle part of a quest chain (incomplete prerequisite)?
