# 👹 Boss Basics & Mechanics

Understand boss fights before you step into the arena. These fundamentals apply to every encounter.

---

## The Boss Fight Framework

Every boss has:

| Element | What It Means |
|---------|---------------|
| **Qi Shield (Blue Bar)** | Boss's defense layer. Must break to deal real damage. |
| **Attack Pattern Set** | Each boss has 4-7 attack types. Learn them. |
| **Exhausted Window** | After Qi break, boss is stunned. Your DPS window. |
| **Enrage (Red Glow)** | Low HP → faster, stronger attacks. Be ready. |

---

## Universal Boss Strategy

```
Phase 1 (Shielded):
  → Block/Dodge attacks
  → Break Qi shield with heavy attacks + Mystic Arts

Phase 2 (Exhausted, Qi broken):
  → DUMP ALL BURST
  → Debuff the boss (Vulnerable, bleed)
  → Before Qi regenerates: reposition

Phase 3 (Enrage, low HP):
  → Play safe
  → Use defensive cooldowns
  → Chip away, don't greed
```

---

## Boss Attack Types

| Attack | Tell | Response |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Light Combo** | Quick windup, no glow | Block (tank) or dodge (DPS) |
| **Heavy Slam** | Slow windup, weapon raised | Perfect Dodge → counter |
| **Grab** | Red glow, lunging animation | Dodge sideways. Do NOT block. |
| **AoE Explosion** | Circle expanding on ground | Dodge out. Do NOT block. |
| **Projectile** | Ranged attack, visible projectile | Block or sidestep |
| **Charge** | Boss repositions, linear path | Sidestep. Don't be in the line. |

---

## Party Role Tips

| Role | Priority |
|------|----------|
| **Tank** | Face boss away from party. Block, don't dodge. Taunt during Exhausted. |
| **Melee DPS** | Flank the boss. Never stand in front. Save burst for Exhausted. |
| **Ranged DPS** | Stay at max range. Kite if targeted. |
| **Healer** | Position between tank and DPS. Heal proactively — before damage, not after. |

---

## Wipe Prevention Checklist

Dying too much? Check:

- [ ] Is my gear at appropriate tier for this boss?
- [ ] Am I blocking/dodging the right attacks? (red glow = don't block)
- [ ] Am I wasting burst on shielded boss?
- [ ] Am I standing in AoE indicators?
- [ ] Is my healer in range?
- [ ] Have I upgraded my Inner Arts?

---

> **Golden rule:** Dead DPS deals zero damage. Survive first, optimize damage second.
