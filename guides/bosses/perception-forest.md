# 👹 Perception Forest Bosses

The Battle Royale zone has its own boss encounters. Win here for exclusive rewards.

---

## What Is Perception Forest?

Where Winds Meet's **Battle Royale mode**:
- Enter via matchmaking or solo queue
- 20+ players, shrinking zone
- PvPvE — players AND bosses
- Bosses drop exclusive gear and Karma Points (Midnight Blades sect)

---

## Perception Forest Boss Encounters

### Shadehound (Early Game)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Forest outskirts, spawns after 2 minutes |
| **Difficulty** | ★★☆☆☆ |
| **Drop** | Perception Box (consumables + temp buffs) |
| **Strategy** | Quick kill, low HP. But other players WILL come. Kill it fast or use it as bait. |

### The Watcher (Mid Game)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Central ruins, spawns after 5 minutes |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Drop** | Rare gear piece + Perception Key |
| **Attacks** | Eye beam (cone, high damage), summon watcherlings, teleport |
| **Strategy** | Hide behind pillars to avoid beam. Kill watcherlings first — they heal the boss. Save burst for the last 30% HP. |

### Forest Avatar (Final Boss)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Center of final circle |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★★ |
| **Drop** | Legendary gear + exclusive cosmetics |
| **Phases** | 3, with zone-shrinking between phases |
| **Attacks** | Root grab (AoE), vine sweep (360°), forest's wrath (full-arena channel) |

### Forest Avatar Strategy

```
Phase 1: Stay at range. Poke. Dodge root grabs (ground cracks before they pop).
Phase 2: Adds spawn. Kill them — they buff the boss if alive.
Phase 3: Burn phase. Boss channels forest's wrath — interrupt or hide behind
         the glowing tree. After channel, boss is exhausted → dump everything.
```

## Perception Forest Survival Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| **Land near a boss spawn** | Early Shadehound = free loot before other players arrive |
| **Don't fight boss mid-zone** | Other players will third-party you |
| **Use the boss as bait** | Hide nearby. Let someone else engage, then clean up both. |
| **Prioritize players over boss** | Boss loot means nothing if you're dead |
| **Save mobility skills** | Cloud Steps / Addled Mind for escaping bad boss + player situations |
