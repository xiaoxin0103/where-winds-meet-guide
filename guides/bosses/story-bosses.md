# 👹 Story Bosses (Chapters 1-3)

Every main story boss in Where Winds Meet, with strategies.

---

## Chapter 1: Heaven Has No Pier (Qinghe)

### Boss 1: The Drunken Swordsman
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Harvestfall Village outskirts |
| **Difficulty** | ★★☆☆☆ (Tutorial boss) |
| **Key Attack** | 3-hit sword combo → pause → heavy slam |
| **Strategy** | Block the combo, dodge the slam. Counter during recovery. |

### Boss 2: Moonveil Guardian
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Moonveil Mountain peak |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Key Attack** | AoE ground pound + charge attack |
| **Strategy** | Stay mid-range. Bait the charge → dodge sideways → punish from behind. |

### Chapter 1 Final Boss: The Corrupted Monk
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Sundara Land temple |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Phases** | 2 (normal → enraged at 40% HP) |
| **Key Attack** | Shadow clones (3) + group heal |
| **Strategy** | Kill clones first. Interrupt the heal with Talon Strike. In Phase 2, kite during enrage, burst after it fades. |

## Chapter 2: Universal Furnace (Kaifeng)

### Boss 1: General Ironclaw
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Kaifeng City gates |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Key Attack** | Spear thrust combo + cavalry charge |
| **Strategy** | Block the thrusts. When he mounts up, use Cloud Steps to dodge the charge. Attack the horse first — once dismounted, he's much slower. |

### Boss 2: The Inkbound Scholar
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Palace of Annals |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Key Attack** | Ranged ink projectiles + AoE ink pools on ground |
| **Strategy** | Stay mobile. Don't stand in ink pools. Close the gap quickly — he's weak in melee. |

### Chapter 2 Final Boss: Furnace Keeper
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Grand Forge, Kaifeng |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★★ |
| **Phases** | 3 (fire → lava → enrage) |
| **Key Attack** | Flame breath (cone), lava floor patterns, summon fire elementals |
| **Strategy** | Phase 1: dodge flame breath sideways. Phase 2: stand on safe platforms, avoid lava. Phase 3: burn cooldowns, race to kill before floor is fully covered. |

## Chapter 3: A Lonely Moon Over Chang'an (Hexi)

### Boss 1: Desert Warlord
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Jade Gate Pass |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Key Attack** | Sandstorm blind + ambush from sand |
| **Strategy** | When sandstorm hits, group up (share vision). Use AoE to reveal him when he burrows. |

### Boss 2: Liangzhou Commander
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Liangzhou fortress |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★☆ |
| **Key Attack** | Shield wall + archer volleys |
| **Strategy** | Break shield with heavy attacks. Dodge archer volleys (floor indicators). Kill archers on the walls before engaging the commander. |

### Chapter 3 Final Boss: The Moonlit Shadow
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Qinchuan, final arena |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★★★ |
| **Phases** | 2 (shadow form → true form) |
| **Key Attack** | Teleport strikes, clone army, full-arena AoE |
| **Strategy** | Phase 1: track the real one (clone has no shadow). Phase 2: save defensive cooldowns for the full-arena AoE. Burst during the long recovery after AoE. |
