# 👹 World Bosses

Open-world boss locations, loot tables, and strategies.

---

## What Are World Bosses?

- Roam the open world (not instanced)
- Respawn on a timer (typically 30-60 minutes)
- Can be fought by multiple players simultaneously
- Drop rare gear, upgrade materials, and cosmetics

---

## Qinghe World Bosses

| Boss | Location | Level | Key Drop |
|------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Timberfang** | Northern Bamboo Grove | 15 | Hawkwing weapon piece |
| **Crimson Serpent** | Crimson Cliff caves | 20 | Eaglerise armor piece |
| **Thunderhoof** | Wildmane Ranch | 25 | Rainwhisper weapon piece |

### Timberfang (Giant Wolf)
- **Attacks:** Bite combo, howl (summons 2 wolves), pounce
- **Strategy:** Kill adds immediately. Dodge pounce, punish landing. Easy solo.

### Crimson Serpent
- **Attacks:** Poison spit (AoE pool), tail sweep, constrict (grab)
- **Strategy:** Stay behind it. When it spits, move — the pool persists. If grabbed, ally must stagger it.

### Thunderhoof
- **Attacks:** Charge (linear), stomp (AoE), kick (behind)
- **Strategy:** Don't stand behind or in front. Flank. Sidestep charge. Jump the stomp shockwave.

## Kaifeng World Bosses

| Boss | Location | Level | Key Drop |
|------|----------|-------|----------|
| **Stonehide Behemoth** | Encircling Lake | 30 | Formbend armor piece |
| **Mistveil Wraith** | Mistveil Forest | 32 | Soulshade Umbrella page |
| **Grand Forge Golem** | Grand Forge exterior | 35 | Jadeware weapon piece |

### Stonehide Behemoth
- **Attacks:** Slow heavy slams, roll attack, rock throw
- **Strategy:** Easy to dodge. Long recovery windows. Kill adds that spawn at 50% HP.

### Mistveil Wraith
- **Attacks:** Teleport, fear (AoE), life drain beam
- **Strategy:** When feared, run away from boss. Interrupt drain beam. Bring Meridian Touch to reveal it when invisible.

### Grand Forge Golem
- **Attacks:** Fire AoE, missile barrage, self-destruct sequence at 10% HP
- **Strategy:** Stay mobile during missiles. At 10%, BURN HARD — you have 15 seconds to kill it or wipe.

## World Boss Tips

1. **Check the map for skull icons** — active world bosses are marked.
2. **Group up** — world bosses scale but are easier in parties of 3-4.
3. **Loot is instanced** — everyone gets their own drops.
4. **Last hit doesn't matter** — contribution-based loot.
5. **Set a timer** — 30-60 min respawn. Farm on rotation.
