# 🆕 Version 1.7 Patch Notes & Guide

Latest balance changes, meta shifts, and what matters for your build.

---

## v1.7 Highlights (June 2026)

| Change | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Thundercry Blade buffs** | Stronger charged damage, improved Divine Counter rotation. Now S-tier for PvE. |
| **Stonesplit – Might path tune-up** | Tank builds got stronger overall. More damage, better shield uptime. |
| **Bamboocut – Dust path** | New weapon pairing: Unfettered Rope Dart + Everspring Umbrella. Mobile burst DPS. |
| **Lingering Bone adjustments** | Bleed Inner Arts slightly nerfed in PvP (stacks slower). PvE unchanged. |

---

## Meta Shift Summary

### What Got Better
- **Tank builds (Thundercry Blade + Stormbreaker Spear)** — biggest winner this patch
- **Bamboocut Dust** — new viable build path for Rope Dart players
- **Healers** — minor buffs to Silkbind scaling

### What Got Worse
- **Bleed-heavy PvP builds** — Lingering Bone stacks slower, less pressure
- **Full glass cannon** — defense buffs to tanks indirectly nerfed pure DPS

## What You Should Do

1. **Tank players:** Enjoy the buffs. Your build is the meta now.
2. **PvP players:** Adjust — swap Lingering Bone for Bitter Seasons or Adaptive Steel.
3. **Everyone:** Try Bamboocut Dust. It's new, it's fun, and it's strong.
4. **Farm new gear:** Updated stat weights favor Charged Damage on tanks.
