## ⚠️ Important: Drunken Fist vs. Gauntlets — Clearing the Confusion

Before diving into the Gauntlets, let's clear up a common misconception:

| Ability | Type | What It Actually Is |
|--------|------|---------------------|
| **Drunken Fist (醉拳)** | ✨ Mystic Skill | A special punch ability usable with **any weapon**. Not a weapon type, not a combat path. |
| **Gauntlets (手甲)** | ⚔️ Weapon Type | A brand-new weapon category launching with Hidden Mountain. Has its own combat path, skill tree, and gear. |
| **Drunken Flowers (醉花阴)** | 🏯 Sect | The charm/romance sect (also called Velvet Shade/Floral Reverie). Uses **Vernal Umbrella**, has nothing to do with brawling. |

> **Bottom line:** If you want to punch things as your primary combat style, Gauntlets — not Drunken Fist — is what you're waiting for.

## The Mohist Connection

The Gauntlets are thematically tied to the **Mohist Sect (墨家机关宗)**, the new faction introduced in Hidden Mountain. The Mohists were a real philosophical school in ancient China, famous for:

| Mohist Principle | How It Translates to Gauntlets |
|------------------|-------------------------------|
| **"Universal Love" (兼爱)** | Defensive counter-play — you don't strike first, you respond |
| **"Non-Aggression" (非攻)** | The weapon's core loop is counter-attack, not initiation |
| **Engineering Mastery** | Mechanical gauntlets with eagle-automaton companions |
| **Frugality & Utility** | No flashy flourishes — every movement has a purpose |

The Mohist sect's hidden valley, **Hui Valley (晦谷)**, serves as an industrial workshop where these mechanical gauntlets were forged. The story suggests the Gauntlets were originally **defensive tools** repurposed for combat.

## Combat Path: Bamboocut — Kite (破竹·鸢)

"Kite" (鸢) refers to the eagle companion that fights alongside you — a mechanical Mohist automaton that follows up your attacks.

### Core Combat Loop

```
Dodge Incoming Attack → Counter-Strike → Eagle Follow-Up
       → Pursuit Dash → Charged Finisher → Repeat
```

| Phase | Input | What Happens | Eagle Role |
|-------|-------|-------------|------------|
| **1. Evade** | Dodge / Sidestep | Quick sidestep with i-frames; builds "Momentum" stack | Eagle circles, marking the enemy |
| **2. Counter** | Light Attack after dodge | Fast jab that interrupts enemy combo; consumes 1 Momentum | Eagle dives for bonus damage |
| **3. Flurry** | Light Attack chain (3-5 hits) | Rapid strikes, fastest attack speed in the game | Eagle pecks between your hits |
| **4. Pursuit** | Dash + Heavy Attack | Close distance instantly; stagger on hit | Eagle binds enemy (1s root) |
| **5. Finisher** | Charged Heavy (hold) | **"Iris Break" (破鸢)** — devastating punch; consumes all Momentum stacks for bonus damage | Eagle screech AoE (fear nearby enemies) |

### Momentum System

Gauntlets use a **Momentum stack** mechanic that rewards patient, defensive play:

| Momentum Stacks | How to Build | Bonus Effect |
|:---:|---|---|
| 1 | Successful dodge | +5% counter damage |
| 2 | Perfect dodge (last-frame) | +10% counter damage, eagle attack speed up |
| 3 | Counter-hit an attacking enemy | +15% counter damage, next finisher gains i-frames |
| 4 | Dodge 3 consecutive attacks | +20% counter damage, finisher becomes unblockable |
| 5 (Max) | Perfect dodge + counter + pursuit combo | **"Mohists' Resolve"** — finisher deals 300% damage, full i-frame duration |

> Momentum decays after **4 seconds** of not dodging or countering. This enforces the "wait, then strike" Mohist philosophy — you can't just stockpile stacks passively.

## FAQ

<div class="faq-section">

### Q: Are Gauntlets a paid weapon?

**No.** Like all weapons in Where Winds Meet, Gauntlets are free. You unlock them through the Hidden Mountain story quest. Cosmetics for Gauntlets will be in the cash shop and gacha pools, but the weapon itself and its combat path are free.

### Q: Do I need to join the Mohist Sect to use Gauntlets?

No. While the Gauntlets are thematically Mohist, they function as a standard weapon type available to any character regardless of sect affiliation. However, Mohist sect passives may provide Gauntlets-specific bonuses.

### Q: Can I use Drunken Fist WITH Gauntlets?

**Yes!** Drunken Fist is a Mystic Skill (秘技) — a special ability slot separate from your weapon. You can equip Gauntlets as your weapon AND slot Drunken Fist as your Mystic Skill for a full unarmed-brawler fantasy.

### Q: Are Gauntlets good for PvE boss fights?

Yes, but with a skill requirement. Bosses with telegraphed attacks are ideal — you can dodge and counter reliably. Chaos fights with multiple enemies are harder because Momentum decays faster when you can't focus on one attacker's patterns.

### Q: When exactly do Gauntlets release?

No exact date yet — the Hidden Mountain expansion is confirmed for "late July 2026." Industry speculation points to July 22-24 (before the Lingyin Pool warm-up on July 24) or July 29-31.
