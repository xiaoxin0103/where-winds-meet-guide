## Tips for Qinchuan

1. **Bring water skins** — desert heat damages you over time. Buy them from Oasis Town vendors.
2. **Meridian Touch is essential** — too many invisible enemies and hidden paths.
3. **Upgrade your mount** — Qinchuan is huge. Fast travel points are sparse.
4. **Night exploration is dangerous** — stronger enemies spawn after dark. Bring a party.
