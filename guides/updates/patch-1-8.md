## 🏡 Homestead System — Rebuild Blissful Retreat

The headline feature sends you back to **Qinghe** to rebuild the village where your character grew up — now burned to ruins.

### Core Gameplay Loop

```
Recruit Villagers → Construct Buildings → Generate Currency → Expand & Upgrade
```

| Step | What You Do |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Recruit** | Find NPCs around Qinghe willing to work. Some require quests or reputation. |
| **2. Build** | Place buildings on your land plot — Inns, Kilns, Breweries, Farms. |
| **3. Collect** | Buildings generate Homestead-exclusive currency over time (3–12 hour cycles). |
| **4. Upgrade** | Spend currency to expand, unlock new building types, and decorate. |
| **5. Share** | "Beyond Artistry" (July 2026) lets you showcase your residence to other players. |

### Building Types

| Building | Produces | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Inn** | Steady coin income | Passive gold, beginner-friendly |
| **Porcelain Kiln** | High-value crafted goods | Max profit (longer cycles) |
| **Wine Brewery** | Consumable buffs + coin | Combat preparation |
| **Crop Farm** | Ingredients, fast cycles | Active players (short cooldowns) |
| **Fishing Dock** | Cooking materials | Food buffs for raids |

### Progression Tips

1. **Start with Crop Farms** — shortest cycles, quickest ROI to bootstrap your economy.
2. **Recruit high-tier villagers first** — better workers = faster production.
3. **Time your collections** — log in every 3–6 hours for optimal farm yields; check every 12 hours for kilns/breweries.
4. **Free outfit unlocked**: Complete the Homestead tutorial to claim the **Summer's Blush Outfit**.

### Homestead Shop

Spend Homestead currency on:
- Exclusive furniture & decorations
- Crafting materials
- Companion items (Fluffballs, Deeds of Fellowship)
- Cosmetic blueprints

|----------|-------------|

## ⚔️ Martial Path Domain — 5-Player Gauntlet

A challenging 20-floor PvE dungeon for coordinated teams.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Event Period** | June 25 – July 23, 2026 |
| **Requirement** | Level 60+ |
| **Team Size** | 5 players |
| **Floors** | 20 (Normal + Hard difficulty) |
| **Hard Mode Unlock** | Clear Normal once |

### Floor Mechanics

- **Three challenges per floor.** Complete 1 to pass, 2 to skip +2 floors, all 3 to skip +3 floors.
- **Key floors (5, 10, 15, 20):** Must be cleared directly — no skipping.
- **Dungeon types rotate:** Kill Enemies (waves), Boss Challenge (timed), Target Protection (defend NPC).

### Mist Effects (Higher Floors)

| Mist | Effect | Strategy |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Goosefall** | AoE damage procs (helpful) | Group enemies, let it work |
| **Thorn Armor** | Enemies reflect 30% damage | Bring healers, pace DPS |
| **Vitality Drain** | Gradual HP loss | Silkbind healer mandatory |
| **Enrage Timer** | Reduced time limit | Optimize DPS rotation |

### Martial Spirits (Mode-Exclusive Buffs)

Six types drop inside the domain: **Heaven, Earth, East, South, West, North.** Each grants unique combat buffs. Recycle unwanted Spirits for shards → exchange for better ones at the Spirit Merchant.

**Best Spirit priority:** Heaven (raw damage) > South (crit) > East (speed).

## 🪦 Tomb Raiders — PvPvE Extraction

A casual, free-form looting mode set in ancient caves.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Type** | PvPvE (player-vs-player-vs-environment) |
| **Duration** | Limited to Version 1.8 cycle |
| **Gameplay** | Explore tombs, kill enemies, loot treasures, fight other players |

### How It Works

1. Enter the tomb instance (solo or duo queue).
2. Fight PvE enemies guarding treasure rooms.
3. Compete with other players who may ambush you.
4. Extract with loot to earn **event points**.
5. Spend points at the Tomb Raiders event shop.

### Key Reward

> 🐎 **Rare Purple Mount** — exchanged with event points. **Time-limited to v1.8.** Don't miss it!

### Survival Tips

- Stick to edges of the map early — avoid center ambushes.
- Listen for footsteps and combat sounds (other players fighting = opportunity or danger).
- Extract early with partial loot rather than greed for max and lose everything.
- Bring a mobility weapon setup (Rope Dart + Umbrella) for quick escapes.
