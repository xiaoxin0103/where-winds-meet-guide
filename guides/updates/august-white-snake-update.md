## 🍶 New Martial Art #1: Drunken Fist (醉拳)

The **Drunken Fist** is one of the most iconic martial arts in Chinese cinema history, and it's finally coming to Where Winds Meet as a playable combat style.

### Core Identity

Drunken Fist is a **feint-heavy, evasive melee style** that rewards misdirection and unpredictable movement. Unlike the straightforward combos of the Nameless Sword or the positional play of the Spear, Drunken Fist is built around making your opponent guess wrong.

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Weapon Type** | Unarmed / Fist (new category) |
| **Range** | Close-quarters melee |
| **Playstyle** | Evasive feint → Burst counter |
| **Skill Ceiling** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Extremely high |
| **Best For** | PvP duels, 1v1 boss fights, style points |
| **Weakness** | Crowd control, ranged pressure, requires excellent timing |

## ⚔️ New Martial Art #2: Dual Blades (双刀)

The second new weapon discipline arriving on August 7 is **Dual Blades** — a fast-paced, dual-wielding style that emphasizes relentless pressure and rapid hit-count stacking.

### Core Identity

If Drunken Fist is about tricking your opponent, Dual Blades is about **overwhelming them**. This is the highest attack-speed weapon in the game, designed for players who want to never stop pressing buttons.

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|

| **Best For** | PvE DPS, mob clearing, breaking guards |

## 🐍 White Snake Legend Collaboration

The White Snake Legend (白蛇传) is one of China's **Four Great Folktales**, alongside *Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai*, *The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl*, and *Meng Jiangnu*. This crossover brings the story's characters, aesthetics, and supernatural atmosphere into Where Winds Meet.

### The Story

For those unfamiliar, the legend tells of **Bai Suzhen** (白素贞), a white snake spirit who transforms into a beautiful woman and falls in love with a human scholar, **Xu Xian** (许仙). A Buddhist monk, **Fahai** (法海), sees the relationship as an abomination and attempts to separate them — leading to the epic *"Flooding of Jinshan Temple"* battle.

### What's Coming in the Crossover

| Content | Description | How to Obtain |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Bai Suzhen (白素贞) Outfit** | Elegant white silk robe with serpent-scale textures and flowing sleeves | Battle Pass / Shop |
| **Xu Xian (许仙) Outfit** | Scholar's robes with umbrella accessory | Event quest reward |
| **Xiaoqing (小青) Companion** | Green snake spirit pet/companion — teases the pet battle system | Event reward |
| **Leifeng Pagoda Dungeon** | Limited-time 5-player instance based on the legend's climax | Available during event |
| **West Lake Boat Scene** | Interactive cutscene recreation of the iconic boat meeting | Story quest |
| **Snake Spirit Mount** | Spectral white snake mount with water-walking ability | Premium / Lucky Draw |
| **"Flooding Jinshan" Weather Event** | Dynamic rain + flood effects in select zones during the event | Server-wide event |

### Supernatural / Horror Theme

The August update is billed as a **Chinese supernatural horror** patch. Alongside the White Snake content, expect:

- **Jiangshi (僵尸)** — hopping vampire enemies in nighttime zones
- **Ghost Market** — special midnight vendor with exclusive items
- **Haunted Locations** — mist-shrouded areas with paranormal ambiance
- **Spirit Photography** — new perception mini-game for capturing ghostly apparitions

This follows the game's established pattern of blending historical authenticity with wuxia fantasy — the July Hidden Mountain expansion leans into Mohist philosophy and engineering, while August pivots to folklore and the supernatural.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="faq-section">

### Q: Is Drunken Fist a weapon or an unarmed style?

**It's the first unarmed combat discipline.** You won't equip a physical "fist weapon" in the traditional sense — instead, Drunken Fist occupies the weapon slot with invisible "Fist Wraps" or "Wine Gourd" items that provide stats. Combat animations are entirely body-based.

### Q: Will Dual Blades count as one weapon or two?

**Dual Blades is a single weapon slot item** — you equip a "Blade Pair" that counts as one weapon for inventory purposes. Individual blades within the pair can be customized cosmetically.

### Q: Can F2P players access all the White Snake content?

The White Snake story quest, Leifeng Pagoda dungeon, and event rewards (including the Xu Xian outfit and Xiaoqing companion) **are free for all players**. Premium cosmetics (Bai Suzhen outfit, snake mount) will be paid or gacha. As always with Where Winds Meet, **no combat power is locked behind payment** — paid content is cosmetic or convenience only.

### Q: Will Drunken Fist and Dual Blades be viable in PvE?

Yes. Based on the developer track record, new weapons are always tuned to be competitive. Dual Blades appears designed specifically for high-end PvE DPS, while Drunken Fist is aimed more at PvP but should perform well in 1v1 boss scenarios.

### Q: Is the White Snake crossover limited-time?

The cosmetics, mount, and dungeon will likely be available for a limited window (typically 4–6 weeks for Where Winds Meet events). The story quest and Xiaoqing companion may become permanent additions, similar to how previous event content has been handled.

### Q: Do I need to finish Hidden Mountain content first?

Not necessarily. The August update is a separate content drop. However, you'll want to be at or near the level cap to access the Leifeng Pagoda dungeon and high-level White Snake quests.
