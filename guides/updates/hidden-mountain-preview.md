## What's Coming

### 🏔️ New Map: Hidden Mountain

The centerpiece of the expansion — a massive new zone built around **vertical exploration**:

- **Multi-layered mountain terrain** — towering cliffs, hidden caves, suspended walkways, and deep ravines
- Development team emphasizes **"风、光、石"** (wind, light, stone) as the three design pillars
- Designed to push the game's climbing, gliding, and traversal systems to their limits
- Secret passages, underground networks, and sky-high shrines reward exploration

> *"The team wants players to look up, not just around."* — Everstone Studio interview

### 📜 New Story: The Mohist Faction

The narrative revolves around the **Mohist (墨家)** — an ancient Chinese philosophical school known for:

- **Universal love** (兼爱) and opposition to offensive warfare
- **Engineering and mechanics** — siege weapons, traps, defensive fortifications
- **Logic and mathematics** — puzzles and mechanisms are expected to feature heavily

The Mohists have been hiding in the Hidden Mountain for centuries. Your arrival triggers a chain of events that reveals their secrets — and their enemies.

### 🥊 New Weapon Type: Hand Guard (拳法)

For the first time, Where Winds Meet is adding **unarmed/fist combat**:

| Detail | What We Know |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Hand Guard Discipline (拳法) |
| **Style** | Close-range melee, likely high mobility |
| **Arsenal Path** | TBA (possibly a new path) |
| **Playstyle** | Speculated: Combo-heavy, parry-focused, fast attacks |
| **Release** | Launches with the DLC |

This fills a major gap in the weapon roster — currently there's no pure melee fist weapon. Expect it to compete with Twinblades for close-range dominance.

### 🎮 New Bosses

- Confirmed: high-difficulty bosses designed around the vertical map design
- Likely Mohist mechanical constructs (automaton-style enemies fitting the engineering theme)
- Some bosses may require use of the new Hand Guard weapon

## FAQ

**Q: Is Hidden Mountain free?**
A: Yes. Like all Where Winds Meet expansions, it's free for all players.

**Q: Do I need to finish the main story first?**
A: Probably not required, but progression through Kaifeng (Chapter 1+) is likely expected for the level range.

**Q: Will Hand Guard be available without the DLC?**
A: Yes — the weapon launches alongside the DLC but is part of the base game. You may need to unlock it via Skill Theft or a new sect.

**Q: Is this the "1.9" update?**
A: Not officially numbered yet. v1.8 launched June 25. Hidden Mountain could be v1.9, v2.0, or a separate content patch.

**Q: Any new sects?**
A: Not confirmed, but Mohist Hill (locked sect #10) is the obvious candidate for unlocking.
