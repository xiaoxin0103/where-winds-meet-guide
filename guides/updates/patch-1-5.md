## Raging Tides at a Glance

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Theme** | Military brotherhood, "wine is blood, wine is faith" |
| **Weapons** | Heavenquaker Spear (DPS) + Stormbreaker Spear (Tank) |
| **Location** | Northern Vow Ruins, Qinghe |
| **Role** | Aggressive frontline DPS/Tank |

### Sect Rules

| Rule | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Never retreat** | No backing down in Hero's Realm or Sword Trial. Lose Battle Will if you do. |
| **Weekly drinking** | Maintain alcohol intake. Wine is literally mandatory. |
| **Repay debts with wine** | Life debts settled with drinks, not coin. |

### How to Join

1. Go to **Northern Vow Ruins, Qinghe** (east of Battlecrest Slope)
2. Drink from the pot to teleport to the trial
3. Collect and drink **9 specific wines** from across the world within the time limit
4. Defeat **Xiang Dinghua** in a drinking contest
5. Swear the oath → you're in

| Skill | Effect |
|-------|--------|
