# 🗺️ Hidden Mountain Access & Map Layers Guide — How to Unlock Everything in v2.0

> **Release Date:** July 23, 2026 | **Version:** 2.0 | **Region:** Hidden Mountain (不见山) | **Vertical Depth:** 2,000 metres

Hidden Mountain is not just a map — it is four distinct worlds stacked on top of each other, threaded together by ancient Mohist engineering and tied to one of the most involved unlock questlines Where Winds Meet has ever shipped. This guide covers every step of the access quest, every layer of the vertical map, and every new system that unlocks once you are inside.

## How to Access Hidden Mountain — Full Unlock Questline

Gaining entry to Hidden Mountain is gated behind level, location, and story progression. There is no shortcut — you must complete the full quest chain.

### Prerequisites

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Player Level** | Level 32 or higher |
| **Main Story** | Kaifeng main questline completed (up to "Imperial Shadows") |
| **Starting NPC** | Yingying (盈盈) — Kaifeng city centre, near the Grand Xiangguo Temple |
| **Quest Name** | "Yingying's Invitation" (盈盈之邀) |

### Step-by-Step Unlock Progression

| Step | Quest / Area | What You Do | Key Rewards |
|:---:|------|-------------|-------------|
| **1** | **Yingying's Invitation** | Speak to Yingying in Kaifeng. She gives you a cryptic Mohist token and a map fragment. | Mohist Token (quest item), Map Fragment I |
| **2** | **Misty River Crossing** | Travel to the Misty River borderlands. Navigate through fog-shrouded ravines using lantern markers. Visibility is intentionally poor — follow the sound of bell chimes. | Access to Misty River waypoint |
| **3** | **Cliff Puzzle — The Three Locks** | Solve three elemental cliff-face puzzles (Wind, Stone, Light) to open the ancient gate. Each puzzle requires manipulating environmental objects: wind vanes, stone reflectors, and light prisms. | Gate Key, 2,000 XP |
| **4** | **Heron's Trials (苍鹭试炼)** | A series of five combat-and-platforming trials administered by spectral Heron guardians. Each trial tests a different skill: precision gliding, timed climbing, aerial combat, mechanism activation, and a final boss duel. | Heron's Crest, Hidden Mountain surface access |
| **5** | **Haven Astray — Main Chapter 2** | Progress through the Hidden Mountain main storyline up to and including Chapter 2: "Haven Astray" (世外迷踪). This chapter introduces the Mohist faction, unlocks the Underground Layer, and concludes with the Mechanical Crafting tutorial. | Full Hidden Mountain access, Mechanical Crafting unlocked, Celestial Seize ability |

::: warning Critical Note
You do NOT receive full map access until **Chapter 2: Haven Astray** is completed. Before that, only the Surface Layer is explorable. The Underground, Underwater, and Sky layers remain locked behind story progression.
:::

### Unlock Checklist

- [ ] Reach Level 32+
- [ ] Complete Kaifeng main story ("Imperial Shadows")
- [ ] Accept "Yingying's Invitation" from Yingying in Kaifeng
- [ ] Navigate Misty River and activate the waypoint
- [ ] Solve the Three Locks cliff puzzle (Wind, Stone, Light)
- [ ] Pass all five Heron's Trials
- [ ] Complete Hidden Mountain Main Chapter 2: Haven Astray
- [ ] Claim your 60 free pulls and 3 free outfits from version completion rewards

## Map Layers — The Four Worlds of Hidden Mountain

Hidden Mountain redefines map design in Where Winds Meet. Instead of a flat horizontal sprawl, the region spans **2,000 vertical metres** across **4 layers** containing **14 sub-regions**. Each layer has its own biome, enemy ecosystem, traversal mechanics, and reward pools.

### Layer Overview

| Layer | Altitude Range | Sub-Regions | Theme | Primary Mechanics |
|-------|:---:|:---:|-------|-------------------|
| **Surface Layer** (地表层) | 0 – 600m | 4 | Ruins, misty valleys, abandoned Mohist workshops | Exploration, climbing, puzzle-solving |
| **Underground Layer** (地下层) | 0 – –400m | 4 | Underground palaces, cavern networks, ancient mechanisms | Mechanism activation, darkness navigation, elite combat |
| **Underwater Layer** (水下层) | –200 – –800m | 3 | Submerged Mohist ruins, underwater puzzle chambers | Breath management, current navigation, underwater combat |
| **Sky Layer** (天空层) | 800 – 1,200m | 3 | Mohist City suspended in clouds, floating sky cities, celestial platforms | Aerial traversal, mechanical bird flight, gravity puzzles |

## New Systems Unlocked in Hidden Mountain

### Mechanical Crafting — DIY Vehicle Construction

The headline gameplay system of v2.0. Scavenge parts from defeated automatons and abandoned workshops, then assemble fully functional mechanical vehicles and gadgets.

| Crafting Category | Examples | Required Materials | Combat / Utility Use |
|-------------------|----------|-------------------|----------------------|
| **Combat Contraptions** | Spring-loaded fist extenders, flame-spray gauntlets, grappling arms | Scrap metal, gear components, fire essence | Augment Gauntlets combos |
| **Vehicles** | Pedal-powered gliders, steam carts, mechanical horses | Chassis frame, steam core, wheel assemblies | Zone traversal, mounted combat |
| **Utility Gadgets** | Auto-miners, resource detectors, self-playing instruments | Sensor modules, power cells, refined ore | Resource gathering, puzzle solving |
| **Decorative Machines** | Clockwork pets, animated homestead decorations, music boxes | Clockwork cores, decorative plating, gemstones | Homestead customisation |

### Celestial Seize Ability (天工夺魄)

A new traversal-and-puzzle ability unlocked during Chapter 2: Haven Astray. Celestial Seize lets you **rearrange active mechanisms** in the environment — rotate gear assemblies, redirect steam pipes, realign mirror arrays, and recalibrate gravity platforms.

| Celestial Seize Use | Layer Availability | Example |
|---------------------|:---:|---------|
| Rotate gear assemblies | Underground | Reconnect broken power lines to open sealed doors |
| Redirect steam pipes | Underground, Surface | Overheat locked mechanisms to break them open |
| Realign mirror arrays | Underwater | Focus light beams to activate submerged switches |
| Recalibrate gravity platforms | Sky | Shift floating platform positions to create new paths |

### Divinecraft Quest Series (天工奇谭)

An extended questline that teaches the logic behind Mohist mechanisms. Unlike standard side quests, Divinecraft quests are **engineering puzzles** — you must understand how gears, pulleys, counterweights, and steam pressure interact to progress.

| Quest Chapter | Puzzle Type | Reward |
|:---:|---|------|
| 1: First Gear | Basic gear ratios and rotation direction | Mechanical Crafting starter kit |
| 2: Pressure Points | Steam pressure and valve management | Steam Core blueprint |
| 3: Light Paths | Mirror and prism alignment | Celestial Seize upgrade: range +15m |
| 4: Counterbalance | Weight distribution and pulley systems | Sky Layer elevator access |
| 5: The Grand Design | Multi-system integration (all previous mechanics combined) | Legendary mechanical bird skin + title: "Master Artificer" |

### Gear Tuning Overhaul — Modulating Stones

A new endgame system for min-maxers. **Modulating Stones** (调谐石) allow you to reset and reroll individual attribute lines on Tier 100 Epic and Legendary gear.

| Modulating Stone Type | Effect | Acquisition |
|------------------------|--------|-------------|
| **Basic Modulating Stone** | Rerolls 1 random attribute | Crafted from 5x Mohist Scrap + 1x Refined Essence |
| **Precision Modulating Stone** | Rerolls 1 selected attribute (cost increases per reroll) | World boss drops, Divinecraft Chapter 4 reward |
| **Master Modulating Stone** | Rerolls all attributes simultaneously | Clockwork Dragon raid drop, rare event reward |
| **Locking Stone** | Locks 1 attribute from being rerolled | Weekly quest reward, premium shop |

::: tip Tuning Strategy
Always lock your best attribute before using Precision or Master stones. The cost of rerolling the same attribute increases exponentially — plan your tuning order before spending stones.
:::

## FAQ

<div class="faq-section">

### Q: Can I skip the Heron's Trials?

**No.** The Heron's Trials are mandatory story content. However, they scale to your level, so over-levelling will not trivialise them — the platforming sections are mechanical skill checks, not stat checks.

### Q: What happens if I fail a Celestial Seize puzzle?

Nothing punitive. Failed mechanisms reset after 30 seconds. There is no death penalty, durability loss, or resource cost for failing puzzles.

### Q: Do I need the Mohist Hill Sect to access Sky Layer content?

No. All layers and systems are accessible regardless of your chosen sect. Mohist Hill members receive discounted crafting costs and an extra Divinecraft side-quest, but non-Mohist players can experience everything.

### Q: Are the 60 free pulls time-limited?

Yes. The free pull event runs from July 23 to August 23, 2026. Unclaimed pulls expire after the event ends.

### Q: Can I hunt Mechanical Marvels in a group?

Yes. Marvels are open-world spawns and any player who tags them receives loot. The Clockwork Qilin is a server-wide event — coordinate with your server community for the best chance.

### Q: How long does the full unlock take?

Expect 8–12 hours of focused play to go from "Yingying's Invitation" to full Sky Layer access. Casual players should budget 15–20 hours spread across multiple sessions.
