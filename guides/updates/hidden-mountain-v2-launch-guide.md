## Hand Guard (拳甲) — New Weapon Guide

The Hand Guard is the game's first pure **close-range brawler weapon**, filling a gap that long-range weapons couldn't cover. Based on the Chinese community's PVP testing in Season 3:

### Core Mechanics

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Range** | Melee (shortest in game) |
| **Playstyle** | Aggressive rushdown + control chain |
| **Strength** | Pseudo-invincibility frames on engage, multi-stage CC chain |
| **Weakness** | Vulnerable to kite-heavy builds (Kite God, Twinblades) |

### Best Combos

```
Hand Guard Engage → 2-Stage Stagger → Umbrella "Spring Hate" Root →
Dragon Ride + Toad Art → Flame Fist Burst → Execute Finisher
```

## New Map: Hidden Mountain Region

### Geography

The Hidden Mountain is a **vertical zone** unlike any previous map. Instead of sprawling horizontally like Kaifeng or Hangzhou, it stacks content vertically across multiple layers:

| Layer | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Summit** | Mohist mechanical city, main quest hub |
| **Mid-Mountain** | Clockwork ruins, puzzle dungeons, world bosses |
| **Forest Floor** | Bamboo groves, hidden shrines, resource nodes |
| **Underground** | Ancient Mohist forges, elite enemy camps |

### Rideable Mechanical Birds

These function as both transport and combat tools:
- **Horizontal flight** for crossing canyons
- **Vertical ascent** to reach cliffside shrines
- **Combat dive** to initiate aerial attacks on ground enemies
- Crafted using Mohist components found throughout the region

## New Enemies: Clockwork Automatons

### Enemy Types

| Enemy | Threat Level | Behavior |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Sentinel Puppet** | Low | Slow, predictable patrolling |
| **Blade Automaton** | Medium | Fast spinning blade arms, bleed damage |
| **Siege Construct** | Elite | Massive AoE stomps, must break legs first |
| **Mohist Mastermind** | Boss | Summons puppet squads, environmental traps |

### Puppet-Fighter Squads

A new enemy coordination system: clockwork enemies now fight in **synchronized formations** with designated tank, DPS, and support roles. Breaking their formation (by killing the support unit first) causes the squad to lose coordination bonuses.

**Strategy:**
1. Identify the **commander puppet** (glowing red core)
2. Kill support units first (healing glyph puppets)
3. Focus commander last — it gains enrage when squad dies

## Mohist Hill — New Sect

The Mohist Sect was an actual historical school of Chinese philosophy focused on engineering, logic, and defensive warfare. In-game, they offer:

| Sect Benefit | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Discounted crafting** | Mechanical bird parts, clockwork traps |
| **Exclusive skill** | "Mohist Barrier" — deployable energy shield |
| **Sect armor** | Steam-powered defensive gear with damage reflection |
| **Reputation grind** | Repair ruins + defeat automatons |

### How to Join

1. Reach the Hidden Mountain summit
2. Complete "Echoes of Mozi" quest chain (3 parts)
3. Pass the Mohist Engineering Trial (logic puzzle + combat test)
4. Swear the Mohist Oath

::: tip Pro Tip
Bring a ranged weapon for the Engineering Trial — the final combat test features flying clockwork drones that melee builds struggle to hit.
:::

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to finish the Imperial Palace (v1.7) content first?**
A: Yes, the Hexi campaign (chapters 1-3) and Imperial Palace main quest are prerequisites for Hidden Mountain access.

**Q: Is Hand Guard viable for PVE or just PVP?**
A: Excellent in both. The CC chain works on elite enemies, and the pseudo-invincibility frames make boss fights much more forgiving.

**Q: Can I switch to Mohist Hill if I'm already in another sect?**
A: Yes. Leave your current sect, wait 24 hours cooldown, then join Mohist Hill. You lose reputation but keep all martial arts.

**Q: How big is the download?**
A: Estimated 25-30 GB. This is a full expansion, not a patch.

**Q: Will my Xbox progress sync with PC/Mobile?**
A: Yes, full cross-save support across all platforms.
