## How to Prepare for New Seasons

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Complete all current story content
- [ ] Max out weekly reputation caps
- [ ] Stockpile upgrade materials (don't spend them)
- [ ] Save gold/coins for new gear

### Day 1 of New Season
- [ ] Read patch notes (check official Discord)
- [ ] Try new weapons/sects on a training dummy first
- [ ] Don't dismantle old gear — meta might shift again
- [ ] Check this guide for updated builds
