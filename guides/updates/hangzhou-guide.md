# 🏯 Hangzhou Expansion — Complete Exploration Guide

> **Released July 10, 2026.** Six new zones, hidden dungeon, ship inspections, and a day/night dual-state wasteland. This guide covers everything.

---

## Overview

The Hangzhou expansion opens the city's "port side" — a sprawling waterfront district with international trade, criminal underworlds, and ancient secrets. Unlike previous zones, Hangzhou introduces **interactive gameplay mechanics**: ship cargo inspection, day/night state changes, and stealth-based investigation.

| Zone | Theme | Key Mechanic |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 浙江渡 | Trade Port | Ship inspection + document verification |
| 海客巷 | Exotic Market | Hidden scam investigation |
| 莽滩口 | Lawless Wasteland | Day/night dual-state |
| 钱塘官衙 | Government District | Quest hub |
| 钱江市井 | Marketplace | Trading, vendors |
| 镇远镖局 | Escort Agency | Courier missions |

## 🚢 浙江渡 (Zhejiang Ferry) — Trade Port

### How Ship Inspection Works

<div class="hero-card s-plus">

**New Mechanic: 入港稽查 (Port Entry Inspection)**

1. Talk to the **harbor master** to receive inspection warrants
2. Board docked ships and examine **cargo manifests**, **crew documents**, and **hull markings**
3. Cross-reference documents with port records — look for discrepancies
4. Confront suspicious merchants or report them to authorities
5. **Rewards:** Gold, reputation, and rare trade goods

</div>

### Key NPCs

| NPC | Location | Role |
|-----|----------|------|
| Harbor Master Zhou | Main dock | Gives inspection quests |
| Merchant Liu | Warehouse district | Suspicious cargo — investigate him |
| Inspector Chen | Government office | Turns in completed warrants |

### Collectibles

- **3 Ship Logs** — Hidden in cargo holds, tell the backstory of a smuggling ring
- **Port Authority Seals (×5)** — Scattered across docks, needed for achievement
- **Rare Trade Map** — Complete all inspections to unlock a hidden treasure location

## 🏮 海客巷 (Haike Alley) — Exotic Market

### The Golden Toad Court (金蟾院) Investigation

This is the zone's main questline — a perfume shop hiding a dark secret.

| Phase | What To Do |
|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Browse the perfume shop. Note the owner's evasive answers. |
| 2 | At night, sneak behind the shop. Find the hidden cellar entrance. |
| 3 | Inside: evidence of illegal trade and kidnappings. |
| 4 | Report to authorities OR blackmail the owner (choice matters). |

<div class="callout tip">
<div class="callout-title">💡 Investigation Tip</div>
Talk to the foreign merchants in the market BEFORE investigating. They drop hints about the shop's "special customers" that unlock additional dialogue options.
</div>

### Notable Vendors

| Vendor | Sells |
|--------|-------|
| Persian Merchant | Rare dyes, exotic fabrics |
| Korean Trader | Ginseng, medicinal herbs |
| Japanese Merchant | Weapon skins, katana accessories |

## 🌙 莽滩口 (Mangtan Mouth) — Day/Night Wasteland

### Dual-State Mechanics

| Time | State | What's Available |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| ☀️ **Day** | Refugee camp | Aid quests, coffin boat blessings, NPC dialogue |
| 🌙 **Night** | Black market | Secret deals, contraband trade, hidden NPCs |

### Hidden Dungeon: 幽明古渡 (Netherworld Ancient Ferry)

<div class="hero-card s-plus">

**Access:** Only at night. Find the ferryman at the abandoned dock. Pay with "ghost coins" (found during daytime exploration).

**Boss:** The Ferryman of the Dead — phase-based fight with spirit minions.

**Reward:** Exclusive martial art scroll + Netherworld cosmetic set.

</div>

### Daytime Activities

- Help refugees with supply quests (rep gain)
- Bless coffin boats for karma points
- Gather ghost coins from hidden stashes (needed for dungeon entry)

### Nighttime Activities

- Black market trading (rare items, exclusive gear)
- Secret NPC encounters
- Dungeon access (幽明古渡)
