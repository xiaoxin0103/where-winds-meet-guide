# 🎭 The Masked Troupe: Complete Sect Guide

> **Music. Dance. Freedom.** If you'd rather perform your way through the jianghu than fight through it, this is your stage.

## Sect Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Theme** | Performance, music, dance, artistic expression |
| **Martial Art** | TBD (twindrums hinted, not yet implemented in global release) |
| **Role** | Performer / Social / Support (combat system pending) |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★☆☆ (Easy to join, but weekly popularity grind requires consistency) |
| **Location** | Fairgrounds, Kaifeng City (find Han Yanluo on a small boat west of the central temple) |
| **Key Content** | Spotlight Mode, instrument/dance performances, player Likes system |

The Masked Troupe is the **"bard class"** of Where Winds Meet — a sect built around performance rather than traditional martial arts. Members are known as **Virtuosos**: wandering performers who pursue artistic perfection (the Dao) through creativity, music, and dance.

> **⚠️ Important:** The Masked Troupe's combat martial arts and weapons are currently **not yet implemented** in the global release. This guide covers everything you CAN do — joining, ranking up, earning rewards, and mastering the performance systems. Combat content will be added when the devs release it.

## How to Join

Joining the Masked Troupe is one of the more involved sect initiations, but unlike Velvet Shade, you don't need a partner. Here's the step-by-step:

### Prerequisites

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Online Mode** | Must be in online mode (not offline/single-player) |
| **Reach Kaifeng** | Kaifeng City unlocks during the main story (Chapter 2+) |
| **Performance Emotes** | Have at least one instrument or dance emote unlocked |

### Step-by-Step Initiation

1. **Travel to Fairgrounds, Kaifeng City** — The Fairgrounds are in the northern part of the city, near the water.

2. **Find Han Yanluo** — She's on a **small boat west of the central temple area** in Fairgrounds. Look for an NPC in performer's attire.

3. **Exhaust her dialogue** — Talk to her about the Masked Troupe, its history, and what it means to be a Virtuoso.

4. **Accept the invitation** — When she asks if you're interested, choose **"I'm willing to"** to begin the initiation quest.

5. **Perform!** — Open your emote menu → **Talents tab** → select an instrument or dance. Perform in a public area where other players can see you.

6. **Earn 10 Likes from other players** — This is the gate. Other players must manually "Like" your performance. You need 10 Likes total.

7. **Return to Han Yanluo** — Once you have your 10 Likes, report back to finalize your membership.

### Tips for Getting 10 Likes Fast

| Strategy | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Go to Petalfall Crossing** | The most popular gathering spot for performers — high player traffic |
| **Perform during peak hours** | Evenings and weekends have the most players online |
| **Pick a rare instrument** | Players are more likely to Like unique performances |
| **Ask in World Chat** | A polite "LF Likes at Fairgrounds, will Like back!" goes a long way |
| **Coordinate with friends** | Bring a group and trade Likes |

## Sect Rules (Precepts)

The Masked Troupe demands you maintain the artistic image. Break these rules and an NPC enforcer will appear — you'll need to **use Tai Chi to throw them into the water** to maintain secrecy.

| # | Rule | What It Means |
|---|------|---------------|
| 1 | **No Begging** | Never ask for handouts, coins, or free items from other players |
| 2 | **No Partnership Emotes** | Avoid romantic or partnered gestures (hand-holding, couple poses, etc.) |
| 3 | **No Marriage** | Romance and marriage are forbidden — your art comes first |
| 4 | **Complete Outfit Required** | Never be missing top OR bottom clothing pieces in public |
| 5 | **Weekly Popularity Quota** | Earn **1,000 Performer Popularity points** per week (primarily via Spotlight Mode) |

### Weekly Popularity Guide

Spotlight Mode is your main source of popularity points:

| Activity | Popularity Earned |
|----------|-------------------|
| Spotlight Mode performance (full set) | ~200-300 points |
| Receiving player Likes | ~10-20 per Like |
| Completing performance-related dailies | ~50-100 each |
| Participating in Troupe events | Varies |

> **Pro tip:** Do Spotlight Mode 4-5 times spread across the week. Don't cram it all on Sunday night — performance quality affects your score, and tired fingers make bad music.

## Ranks & Rewards (5 Tiers)

The Masked Troupe has a 5-tier rank system. You climb by earning popularity and completing Troupe-related activities.

| Rank | Title | Key Unlocks |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **1** | Junior Musician | **Masked Grace** (Player Avatar), Stage Name Change, Blooming Fireworks, Pose: Butterfly's Tune, Pose: Neon Flag |
| **2** | Standing Musician | **Masked Grace** (Hairstyle), **Masked Grace** (Outfit) |
| **3** | Seated Musician | Reputation Giftbox (random cosmetics and consumables) |
| **4** | Music Officer | Pose: Dancing Petals, Pose: Neon Flag (upgraded) |
| **5** | Music Director | Prestige title (highest rank — bragging rights) |

> **Cosmetics you unlock stay with you even if you leave the sect.** The Masked Troupe is one of the best sects to join temporarily for fashion unlocks, then leave for a combat sect.

### Rank-Up Requirements

| Rank | Requirement (approximate) |
|------|---------------------------|
| Junior → Standing | ~2 weeks of consistent weekly quotas |
| Standing → Seated | ~3-4 additional weeks + Spotlight excellence |
| Seated → Music Officer | ~1 month + Troupe event participation |
| Music Officer → Music Director | Long-term dedication (2+ months), top performer status |

## Key NPCs & Lore

### Hu Changyi — Current Troupe Master

The free-spirited leader of the Masked Troupe. Hu Changyi avoids conflict at all costs and lives by two core tenets:

- **"Seek Truth in Freedom"** — Art cannot flourish under rigid rules. True expression requires liberty.
- **"Seek Abundance in Simplicity"** — The greatest performances come from honest emotion, not elaborate technique.

He's often found near the performance stage at Fairgrounds, offering advice and Troupe quests.

### Gongsu Da Niang — Second Troupe Master (Historical)

A legendary dancer from the **Tang Dynasty**, famous for incorporating **bladed weapons** into her performances. Her signature routine — dancing with twin swords — was said to be so mesmerizing that audiences forgot they were watching a martial display.

After the **An Lushan Rebellion** (755-763 AD), Gongsu Da Niang led the Troupe into seclusion to preserve their art. Her legacy hints at what the Masked Troupe's eventual combat style might look like: a **dance-weapon hybrid** blending graceful movement with lethal precision.

### Han Yanluo — Recruitment Officer

Your first contact with the Troupe. She handles new member initiation and introductory quests. Find her on her boat at Fairgrounds.

## Performance Systems

### Instruments

You can unlock and play various traditional Chinese instruments:

| Instrument | Difficulty | How to Unlock |
|------------|------------|---------------|
| **Guqin** (7-string zither) | ★★☆☆☆ | Starter instrument, free |
| **Dizi** (bamboo flute) | ★★★☆☆ | Purchase from Troupe shop |
| **Pipa** (pear-shaped lute) | ★★★★☆ | Troupe rank reward or cash shop |
| **Erhu** (2-string fiddle) | ★★★★☆ | Special event reward |

### Dances

Dance emotes range from simple flourishes to full choreographed routines. Higher-rank dances earn more Likes from spectators.

### Spotlight Mode

Spotlight Mode is the Troupe's signature activity:

1. Enter Spotlight Mode at any performance area (Fairgrounds stage recommended)
2. Choose your act — instrument, dance, or both
3. Other players watch and rate your performance
4. High-rated performances appear on the regional leaderboard
5. Earn popularity points and exclusive rewards

> **Performance quality matters.** The game's rhythm mechanics judge your timing. Practice makes perfect — and better scores = faster rank progression.

## Strategy: Use Masked Troupe as a "Cosmetic Pit Stop"

A popular strategy among veteran players:

1. **Join Masked Troupe early** (as soon as you reach Kaifeng)
2. **Grind to Standing Musician** (Rank 2) — this unlocks the outfit and hairstyle
3. **Leave for a combat sect** (Well of Heaven, Midnight Blades, etc.)
4. **Keep all your unlocked cosmetics**

This gets you the fashion rewards without committing to the long grind to Music Director. The Standing Musician rank takes about 2 weeks of consistent weekly quotas.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Masked Troupe have weapons yet?**
A: No. The sect page hints at "twindrums" but martial arts are listed as TBD. Expected in a future update.

**Q: Is it worth joining if I don't care about performances?**
A: Only as a cosmetic pit stop (see strategy above). For combat-focused players, join, grab the Rank 2 outfit, and leave.

**Q: Can I perform anywhere?**
A: Anywhere in online mode, but designated performance areas (Fairgrounds, Petalfall Crossing) have higher player traffic = easier Likes.

**Q: What happens if I break a sect rule?**
A: An enforcer NPC appears. Use Tai Chi to throw them into the nearest body of water. This is actually a fun mini-game, not a harsh punishment.

**Q: Does Spotlight Mode require real musical skill?**
A: No — it's a rhythm mini-game similar to Guitar Hero or osu!. Timing matters but it's accessible to everyone.

**Q: Will the Masked Troupe get combat weapons in Hidden Mountain?**
A: Not confirmed. The Hidden Mountain expansion focuses on Mohist Hill. Masked Troupe combat is likely a separate future update.
