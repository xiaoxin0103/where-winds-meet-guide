## How to Join

1. Travel to **Sundara Land, Qinghe**
2. Climb to **Halo Peak** — find the **disturbed novice monk**
3. Accept the quest **"To Save by Slaying"**
4. Defeat the specified targets → swear the oath → you're marked for PvP

### Alternative: Skill Theft

- Sanctum: **Midnight Mercy Sanctum** (east of Crimson Cliff, Qinghe)
- Requires **Touch of Death Mystic Art** + Dragon Key
- Stealth-kill guards, reach the master, complete the skill theft mini-game
- **No sect obligations, no PvP flag**

## Rotation Guide

### PvP Opener

```
Addled Mind (gap close) → Calamity's Greed (spin + slash) → Addled Mind (chase)
→ Light Attack ×4 (build Karmic Flame) → Flamelash (Wrathful Form)
→ 5-hit Light Attack spam with CC immunity
```

### Sword Swap Combo

```
Daunting Strike → Shadow Step → Addled Mind
→ Attack or retreat based on whether enemy blocks
```

### Key PvP Tips

1. **Perfect Dodge resets Addled Mind.** Master this — it's the difference between pressure and dead.
2. **Pop Flamelash when they try to CC you.** Fortitude says no.
3. **Serene Breeze is non-negotiable.** Without it, one CC chain = you're done.
4. **Use Yaksha Rush / Talon Strike** to break through blockers.
5. **Fivefold Bleed + Bitter Seasons** — your attack speed stacks these ridiculously fast.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Midnight Blades bad for PvE?**
A: It's **fine** for PvE, but Why would you join a PvP sect to kill mobs? If you're 80%+ PvE, pick another sect.

**Q: Can I turn off PvP flag after joining?**
A: No. Joining Midnight Blades marks you permanently. Other players can and will attack you.

**Q: What happens if I keep dying and losing Karma?**
A: Your sect standing drops fast. At low standing, you lose access to skills and shop items. Don't join unless you can win fights.

**Q: How does it compare to Nine Mortal Ways for PvP?**
A: Midnight Blades = direct combat, raw damage, in-your-face. Nine Mortal Ways = tricks, traps, social chaos. Different flavors of PvP.
