## How to Join

### Method 1: Full Membership (Recommended)

1. Travel to **Harvestfall Village, Qinghe**
2. Find **San Qian'er** — she'll offer the **"Easy Street"** quest
3. Complete the quest and deliver **Ill-gotten Gains** to Zhang Yihao
4. Swear the sect oath → you're in.

### Method 2: Skill Theft (No Oath)

- Reach **Kaifeng** (finish Chapter 1)
- Go to the southern **Well of Heaven Martial Camp**
- Equip **Touch of Death Mystic Art** for stealth assassinations
- Infiltrate the camp, reach the elevated sanctum
- Step into the **blue ring** to steal Thundercry Blade from the camp master

## Thundercry Blade: Core Mechanics

Thundercry Blade is all about **charged attacks, shields, and timing**. You're not fast — you're unstoppable.

### Fighting Spirit

Your most important resource, shared with Stormbreaker Spear.

| Action | Fighting Spirit |
|--------|----------------|
| Use Predator's Shield (Q) | +2 |
| Use Shifting Heaven (Q-alt) | +2 |
| Block an attack | +1 |
| Sunrush Gale (~) | -2 |
| Stage 3 charged attack | -1 (auto) |

### Key Buffs

| Buff | From | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| **Shield** | Q skills | 25% max HP buffer, core survival tool |
| **Fortitude** | Charged skills | Super armor — can't be interrupted |
| **Focus** | Sunrush Gale | Next charge 50% faster (5s window) |
| **Breakthrough** | Shifting Heaven | +30% charged skill damage, free charging (12s) |

## Skill Breakdown

### Predator's Shield (Q)
- 25% HP shield for 8 seconds
- Generates 2 Fighting Spirit
- **Always use on cooldown.** Shield uptime = survival.

### Shifting Heaven (Perception → Q)
- Same shield + 2 Fighting Spirit
- **Also grants Breakthrough** — +30% charged damage for 12s
- Use this version when you're about to dump charged attacks

### Sunrush Gale (~)
- Costs 2 Fighting Spirit
- Pulls enemies in (AoE vortex) → spinning slash
- Grants **Focus** (next charge 50% faster) + **Fortitude**
- Best opener for AoE packs

### Galecloud Cleave (Charged Light Attack)
- Double sweeping slash, wide AoE
- Stage 3: auto-consumes 1 Fighting Spirit for +20% damage
- Unlocks **Light Varied Combo** for follow-up damage

### Avalanche (Charged Heavy Attack)
- Single-target overhead slam, high damage
- Stage 3: +20% damage + knockdown
- Unlocks **Heavy Varied Combo** — devastating finisher

### Divine Counter (Passive)
- Successful block → instant free Stage 3 charge
- This is your **highest DPS move** — block, then punish

## Rotation Guide

### AoE (Trash Packs)

```
Predator's Shield → Sunrush Gale → Galecloud Cleave (Stage 3) → Light Varied Combo
```

1. Shield up for safety
2. Sunrush pulls everything together
3. Stage 3 charge with Focus speed boost
4. Varied Combo to clean up

### Single Target (Bosses)

```
Shield → Block boss attack → Divine Counter (instant Stage 3) → Avalanche → Heavy Varied Combo
```

1. **Block, don't dodge.** Every block is damage.
2. When boss Qi shield breaks (Exhausted): **Shifting Heaven → Avalanche → Heavy Varied Combo** all under Breakthrough
3. Swap to Stormbreaker Spear → **Thunder Shock** to apply Vulnerable → swap back

### Pro Tips

- **Never spend Fighting Spirit on partial charges** — only Stage 3
- **Animation cancel** with block/parry to shorten Varied Combo recovery
- Use **Dragon's Breath + Drunken Poet** combo for fire burst when available
- Keep shield active **at all times** — dead tanks do zero DPS

## FAQ

**Q: Is Well of Heaven good for solo players?**
A: Yes — it's arguably the **best solo sect**. High survivability means you rarely die, and the damage is solid.

**Q: Can I play without Stormbreaker Spear?**
A: You can, but you're leaving damage on the table. The spear adds Vulnerable debuff + tanking tools. Worth learning.

**Q: What's the biggest mistake new Well of Heaven players make?**
A: **Dodging instead of blocking.** Thundercry Blade rewards blocking — it generates Fighting Spirit and triggers Divine Counters. Dodge only unblockable attacks.

**Q: How does Well of Heaven compare to Raging Tides?**
A: Well of Heaven is tankier and more forgiving. Raging Tides (spears) has higher damage but stricter rules (drinking, no retreat). For new players, Well of Heaven is the clear winner.
