## Which Sect Should You Pick?

### 🟢 For Beginners: Well of Heaven

> *"I just started and want to learn the game without constant frustration."*

- **Weapon:** Thundercry Blade — slow heavy swings with shield stacks and crowd control
- **Why it's great for beginners:** Straightforward combat, forgiving tankiness, heroic theme
- **Rules:** Do chivalrous deeds weekly, don't harm fellow disciples, don't hoard excessive wealth
- **Watch out for:** Earnings cap limits how rich you can get

### 🟢 For Support Players: Silver Needle

> *"I love healing teammates and being valuable in group content."*

- **Weapons:** Panacea Fan (healing) + Inkwell Fan (ranged DPS with tornadoes and teleports)
- **Why it's great:** Always in demand for co-op, two distinct playstyles in one sect
- **Rules:** Charge for ALL healing (even rescues), earn 15 likes weekly
- **Watch out for:** The weekly 15-like quota — do it early in the week

### 🔴 For PvP Enthusiasts: Midnight Blades

> *"I live for player-versus-player combat. Open-world duels are my content."*

- **Weapon:** Infernal Twinblades — fast combos, demon mode transformation, multiple dashes
- **Why it's great:** Highest PvP kill potential, Perception Forest Battle Royale synergy
- **Rules:** Defeat wanderers for Karma, kill without mercy, farm Karma in Battle Royale
- **Watch out for:** This is a **lifestyle** sect — every rule pushes more fighting. Casual PvE downtime costs you standing.

### 🟡 For Chaos Lovers: Nine Mortal Ways

> *"I want to scam, prank, and profit. Combat is secondary."*

- **Weapon:** Mortal Rope Dart — hybrid melee/ranged, pull enemies, rodent summons
- **Why it's fun:** Disguise events, underground market, exclusive cosmetics (v1.2 base added Jan 2026)
- **Rules:** Earn Fun Points by tricking people, join Disguise events, don't get caught
- **Watch out for:** Failed cons = -10 Virtue. Leaving costs 50,000 coins.

### 🟡 For Social Players: Velvet Shade

> *"I care about relationships, fashion, and elegance."*

- **Weapon:** Vernal Umbrella — fly-and-shoot aerial style, auto-shoot drone, elegant AoE
- **Why it's unique:** Deepest social/romance systems, strongest cosmetic rewards
- **Rules:** Reveal charm, form bonds, visit Windtail Lounge, embrace multiple romances
- **Watch out for:** Hardest initiation — requires a partner, Level 10, and a 24-hour wait

### 🟢 For Spear Lovers: Raging Tides (NEW v1.5)

> *"I want aggressive frontline combat with spears and a warrior brotherhood."*

- **Weapons:** Heavenquaker Spear (DPS) + Stormbreaker Spear (Tank)
- **Why it's exciting:** Two distinct spear styles, military brotherhood theme, brand new
- **Rules:** Never retreat in Hero's Realm, maintain weekly drinking, repay debts with wine
- **Watch out for:** Community knowledge is still developing (released April 2026)

## Should You Go Sectless?

Staying **sectless** is a legitimate choice, especially while learning:

| Sectless Pros | Sectless Cons |
|---------------|---------------|
| Total freedom, no weekly quotas | Martial arts training costs more |
| Extra XP from open world | Miss sect-exclusive cosmetics |
| Can join any sect later | No sect community/events |
| Unique sectless cosmetics | Slower reputation progression |

> **Recommendation:** Stay sectless for your first 5-10 hours while you learn the map and combat basics. Then join Well of Heaven when you're ready.

## Changing Sects: What You Lose

- **Sect rank resets** to zero
- **Shop access** lost
- **Most require a Severance Trial** (Perception Forest match)
- **Nine Mortal Ways** just charges 50,000 coins to leave

> You can rejoin a sect later, but everything starts from scratch.

## Skill Theft: Learn Without Joining

Every sect's martial art can be learned through **Skill Theft** without joining. The trade-off:

| Joining | Skill Theft |
|---------|-------------|
| Free/low cost training | Much more expensive |
| Access to sect cosmetics | No cosmetics |
| Weekly reputation rewards | No reputation |
| Sect rules and quotas | Total freedom |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join multiple sects at once?**
A: No. One sect at a time.

**Q: Which sect does the most damage?**
A: Midnight Blades (Infernal Twinblades) has the highest burst. Raging Tides (Heavenquaker Spear) is close behind for sustained DPS.

**Q: Which sect is best for solo players?**
A: Well of Heaven. Tanky, self-sufficient, straightforward.

**Q: Is Velvet Shade worth the complex initiation?**
A: Yes, if you care about cosmetics, social systems, and a truly unique playstyle. Skip it if you just want to fight.

**Q: When will the locked sects release?**
A: No official dates. Previous pattern suggests 1-2 new sects per major patch (every ~3 months).
