## How to Join

### ⚠️ The Hardest Initiation in the Game

Velvet Shade requires **another player**. You cannot join purely solo. Here's the confirmed method:

### Step-by-Step

1. **Reach Level 10** and arrive at **Kaifeng**
2. Team up with a player in **Online Mode**
3. Fast travel to **Crimson Cliff, Qinghe** → talk to **Shi Yimo**
4. Form a **partnership** (requires Intimacy at least **"Companions"** level)
5. Enter the partnership **oath**
6. Talk to Shi Yimo again → **sever the partnership**
7. Wait **24 hours** (real time) for the cooldown
8. Return, pick a severance method → **Velvet Shade invitation** appears in your menu

> 💡 **Workaround for solo players:** You don't need to join the sect to use Vernal Umbrella. You can unlock it via **Skill Theft at Time Tower** or from **Qi Sheng** (first Oddity reward). You miss the cosmetics and Everspring Umbrella, but you get the weapon.

| Full Membership | Skill Theft |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Access to Everspring Umbrella (exclusive) | Vernal Umbrella only |
| Windtail Lounge + sect cosmetics | No sect perks |
| Shadows in Bloom questline | No questline |
| **Must maintain social obligations** | **Total freedom** |
| Flower Points + reputation system | No reputation tracking |

## Vernal Umbrella: Core Mechanics

The Vernal Umbrella is a **ranged/aerial hybrid weapon** unlike anything else in Where Winds Meet. You fly, you shoot, and your umbrella becomes an autonomous turret.

### The Flight Mechanic

Your defining trait: **Spring Away** (charged light attack) launches you into the air while firing projectiles downward. **Melee enemies cannot hit you while airborne.**

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Spring Away | Rise into air, fire projectiles, evade melee attacks |
| Apricot Heaven (charged) | Fly up → deal AoE damage → follow-up slam |
| Aerial combos | Chain Spring Away → Apricot Heaven → Unfading Flower for continuous air pressure |

### Blossom Resource

| Action | Blossom Change |
|--------|---------------|
| Spring Sorrow hits | +10 Blossom |
| Apricot Heaven | +15 Blossom |
| Spring Away hits | +5 per projectile |
| Unfading Flower activation | -50 Blossom |

> Blossom is your secondary resource. Build it with basic skills, spend it on Unfading Flower (your highest DPS ability).

### Key Buffs & Debuffs

| Name | From | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| **Immobilize** | Spring Sorrow | Freezes regular enemies for 3 seconds |
| **Blossom Barrage** | Inner Art (Shadows in Bloom) | Extra Spring Sorrow charge + enemies take increased damage |
| **Airborne** | Spring Away / Apricot Heaven | Melee immunity while in air |
| **Jadewind Shield** | Inner Art | Shield on Spring Away, blocks ranged attacks |

## Skill Breakdown

### Spring Sorrow (Q)
- Fires a flying petal projectile
- **Immobilizes regular enemies for 3 seconds**
- Generates +10 Blossom
- With Blossom Barrage Inner Art: **extra charge** (2 uses before cooldown)
- Your opener and your crowd control — always use first

### Apricot Heaven (Charged Skill)
- Fly into the air dealing AoE damage
- Follow up with a **downward slam** for additional AoE
- Generates +15 Blossom
- Use after Spring Sorrow to maintain air pressure
- **Cancels melee tracking** — most boss melee swings whiff when you're airborne

### Spring Away (Charged Light Attack)
- Rise into the air while firing projectiles
- Each projectile hit: +5 Blossom
- Grants **melee immunity** while airborne
- Chain into Apricot Heaven or Unfading Flower
- Your primary kiting tool against melee enemies

### Unfading Flower (Special — Perception key)
- **Costs 50+ Blossom** to activate
- Umbrella detaches and **auto-fires projectiles** independently
- Extends airborne duration of suspended enemies
- Lasts 10 seconds, fires ~2 projectiles/second
- This is your **highest sustained DPS ability** — save Blossom for it

### Petal Storm (~ skill)
- AoE petal whirlwind around you
- Generates Blossom per enemy hit
- Pushes back melee enemies
- Emergency button when surrounded

### Divine Counter Variant: Petal Parry
- Block → umbrella opens, reflecting the next projectile
- Reflected projectiles deal **150% damage** back to sender
- Also generates +10 Blossom
- Essential against ranged bosses and PvP archers

## Rotation Guide

### PvE: Single Target (Bosses)

```
Spring Sorrow (immobilize + debuff) → Spring Away (airborne + build Blossom)
→ Apricot Heaven (air damage) → Spring Sorrow again (2nd charge)
→ Unfading Flower (dump 50 Blossom, auto-turret) → Petal Storm
→ Swap to Panacea Fan → heal if needed → swap back → repeat
```

### PvE: AoE (Mob Packs)

```
Spring Sorrow (immobilize priority target) → Apricot Heaven (AoE slam)
→ Petal Storm (AoE pushback + Blossom) → Unfading Flower (cleanup)
```

### PvP: Kite & Pressure

```
Spring Sorrow (immobilize) → Spring Away (airborne, evade melee)
→ Apricot Heaven → Unfading Flower → stay airborne
→ Petal Parry (reflect ranged attacks)
→ If they close distance: Soaring Spin away → Spring Away again
```

### Pro Tips

- **Never fight on the ground against melee.** Your entire survival depends on staying airborne. If you're on the ground for more than 3 seconds, you're positioning wrong.
- **Blossom management is everything.** Don't waste Unfading Flower at 50 Blossom if you could build to 100 and chain two back-to-back.
- **Spring Sorrow's immobilize works on most non-boss enemies.** In open-world elite fights, you can chain-CC them with proper timing.
- **Petal Parry reflects ALL projectiles, not just arrows.** Boss magic bolts, PvP Mystic Arts — if it's a projectile, you can reflect it.
- **Panacea Fan swap is for survival, not DPS.** Don't force it into your rotation — only swap when you or a teammate needs healing.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Velvet Shade worth the painful initiation?**
A: Yes — if you enjoy ranged combat, aerial gameplay, and the social systems. Skip it if you want to hit things with a sword and ignore relationships.

**Q: Can I join without another player?**
A: No. The confirmed method requires a partner to form + sever an oath. However, you can get Vernal Umbrella via Skill Theft at Time Tower without joining.

**Q: What's the biggest mistake new Velvet Shade players make?**
A: **Fighting on the ground.** Spring Away should be your default state in combat. If you're standing still firing Spring Sorrow without going airborne, you're playing wrong.

**Q: How does Vernal Umbrella compare to Thundercry Blade for PvE?**
A: Thundercry Blade face-tanks and does burst. Vernal Umbrella kites and does sustained DPS. Against mobile bosses, Umbrella is better. Against stationary bosses, Blade out-damages it.

**Q: Is Everspring Umbrella worth the sect grind?**
A: Yes — it adds AoE Control to your kit (Bamboocut – Dust path) and is S-tier in PvP when paired with Unfettered Rope Dart. The combo of Everspring Umbrella + Unfettered Rope Dart is one of the strongest PvP builds in v1.8.

**Q: Do I really need to do the Shadows in Bloom quest?**
A: Yes. Blossom Barrage (the Inner Art from that quest) doubles your Spring Sorrow charges and adds a damage-taken debuff to enemies. Without it, you're missing ~30% of your potential.
