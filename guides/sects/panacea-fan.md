# 🏥 Silver Needle: Complete Sect & Fan Combat Guide

> **Best for support players. Heal teammates. Control crowds. Always in demand for group content.**

---

## Sect Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Theme** | Medicine, healing, "charge for every cure" |
| **Martial Arts** | Panacea Fan (Healing) + Inkwell Fan (Ranged DPS) |
| **Arsenal Path** | Silkbind – Deluge (Heal) / Silkbind – Jade (DPS) |
| **Role** | Healer / Ranged Support |
| **Difficulty** | ★★★☆☆ (Easy to play, moderate to master) |
| **Location** | Moonveil Mountain → Sage's Knoll, Qinghe |
| **NPC Leader** | Dr. Yuan (Sun Yuan) |

### Sect Rules

| Rule | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Charge for ALL healing** | Even if someone's dying in front of you — bill them. It's the sect way. |
| **15 Likes per week** | Earn likes from healed players. If you miss the quota, lose Precept Value. |
| **Heal in multiplayer** | +10 Precept Value per successful co-op heal. |

> The 15-Like quota is the only real pressure. Do group content early in the week and you're fine.

## How to Join

1. Travel to **Moonveil Mountain, Qinghe**
2. Go to **Sage's Knoll** → find **Dr. Yuan**
3. He'll give you the **"Three Questions"** test:
   - You must successfully heal **3 NPCs** to pass
4. Pass → swear the oath → you're in

### Alternative: Skill Theft

- **Panacea Fan:** Steal from Midnight Mercy Sanctum master
- **Inkwell Fan:** Steal from Inkwell Fan Sanctum
- Use **Touch of Death Mystic Art** + Dragon Keys for infiltration
- No sect obligations, but training costs more

## Rotation Guide

### Healer Mode (Group Content)

```
Position behind team → Cloudburst Healing (AoE) → Emerald Dewtouch (spot heal)
→ Light Dust After Morning Rain (buff low-HP ally) → Jadewind Shield (reposition)
```

- Always stay at mid-range — close enough to heal, far enough to avoid melee
- Save **Resurrection** for critical moments (boss fights, raid wipes)
- Pop heals **before** big damage, not after

### DPS Mode (Solo / Farming)

```
Peak's Springless Silence → Moon Shatter Spring → Dragon's Breath
→ Jadewind Shield (buff light attacks) → Inkwell Fan ranged spam
→ Ghost Bind (AoE CC) → Drunken Poet (burn explosion finisher)
```

## FAQ

**Q: Is Silver Needle good for solo players?**
A: It's **playable** solo, but shines brightest in multiplayer. If you're 90% solo, consider Well of Heaven instead.

**Q: Do I really have to charge for healing?**
A: In-character, yes. In practice, most players just pay the sect's internal rates. It's roleplay flavor, not a real economic burden.

**Q: Panacea Fan or Inkwell Fan first?**
A: **Panacea Fan first** — healing keeps you alive. Inkwell Fan gives you ranged damage options later.

**Q: Can I revive in PvP?**
A: Yes. Resurrection works in group PvP. A well-timed revive can flip a fight entirely.
