## Part 1: Heavenquaker Spear (DPS Build)

### Core Mechanics

Heavenquaker Spear is about **spin-to-win bleed stacking**. You build a buff tier through consecutive hits, then cash out for massive AoE damage or single-target bleed detonation.

### The Sober Sorrow Buff System

Your Q skill (`Sober Sorrow`) spins the spear for six sweeping strikes. Your buff tier depends on **total hits landed**:

| Tier | Hits | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| **Droplet** | <5 | +10% HP damage |
| **Spring Surge** | ≥5 | +15% HP damage |
| **River Flow** | ≥10 | +20% HP damage, Sweep All applies Bleed + reduces defense |

> **River Flow is your goal every rotation.** At max tier, you're dealing 20% bonus HP damage AND shredding defense. It transforms the weapon.

### Skill Breakdown

| Skill | Key | Cooldown | Effect |
|-------|-----|----------|--------|
| **Sober Sorrow** | Q | 12s | Six spinning strikes; buff tier determined by hit count |
| **Sweep All** | ~ | 1s | Wide circular AoE; at River Flow: +Bleed +Defense reduction |
| **Drifting Thrust** | Hold R | — | Charged flurry of forward thrusts; grants **Fortitude** (flinch only to CC) |
| **Raging Tempest** | Perception → Q | 25s | Ultimate — instant River Flow + 50% attack speed for 8s |

### Inner Arts

| Priority | Inner Art | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| 🥇 Must | **Wolfchaser's Art** | Reduces hit thresholds: Droplet → 4, River Flow → 8. Get River Flow 25% faster. |
| 🥈 Must | **Morale Chant** | +1% damage per stack (5 max). Spinning hits = instant max stacks. |
| 🥉 Core | **Adaptive Steel** | Bonus crit damage on charged skills (Drifting Thrust is charged) |
| 4th | **Fivefold Bleed** | Passive bleed stacks on hit; spins apply it to everything in range |

### Best Weapon Pairing: Strategic Sword

Both share **Bellstrike – Umbra**, creating a closed bleed loop:

```
Strategic Sword: Stack 5x Bleed → detonate for burst
       ↓ swap
Heavenquaker Spear: Convert bleed to River Flow → Sweep All × Bleed synergy
       ↓ swap back
Repeat
```

### Rotation (Boss)

```
1. Open on Strategic Sword → Inner Track Slash → stack 5x Bleed
2. Sword Horizon detonate (consume 5 stacks for burst)
3. TAB swap → Sober Sorrow → reach River Flow (10+ hits)
4. Sweep All on cooldown (Bleed + defense reduction active)
5. Drifting Thrust fillers (Fortitude keeps you safe)
6. Raging Tempest when available → accelerates everything
7. Swap back to Sword, reapply Bleed, repeat
```

### Rotation (AoE Trash)

```
Sober Sorrow → Sweep All → Sweep All → Sweep All
```

Yes, it's that simple. Sober Sorrow into max River Flow, then Sweep All deletes everything in a circle.

## Part 2: Stormbreaker Spear (Tank Build)

### Core Mechanics

Stormbreaker Spear is about **aggro control, damage reduction, and keeping your team alive**. You don't top DPS meters — you make sure no one else dies.

### Skill Breakdown

| Skill | Key | Effect |
|-------|-----|--------|
| **Storm Roar** | Q | Taunts all nearby enemies, forcing them to target you. Generates high threat. |
| **Thunder Shock** | ~ | Knocks target down + applies damage output debuff (-25% damage dealt for 8s) |
| **Shield Wall** | Charged Heavy | Frontal shield; blocks all damage from the front for 2s |
| **Rallying Cry** | Perception → Q | Party-wide 15% damage reduction + 10% attack boost for 12s |

### Inner Arts

| Priority | Inner Art | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| 🥇 Must | **Rock Solid** | +20% max HP. More HP = bigger Storm Roar shield. |
| 🥈 Must | **Iron Fortress** | Blocking reduces cooldown of Thunder Shock by 3s per block |
| 🥉 Core | **Morale Chant** | Damage boost helps hold aggro |
| 4th | **Golden Body** | Shield + Fortitude for emergency survival |

### Best Weapon Pairing: Thundercry Blade

Both share **Stonesplit – Might** (Tank path). This is the **#1 meta tank build in v1.7+**:

```
Thundercry Blade: Shields, charged burst, Fighting Spirit generation
       ↓ swap
Stormbreaker Spear: Taunt → Thunder Shock → Rallying Cry → swap back
```

This pairing is currently **S-tier in both PvE and PvP** (June 2026 meta).

### Rotation (Boss Tanking)

```
1. Open with Thundercry Blade → Predator's Shield → build Fighting Spirit
2. When boss targets a DPS: TAB swap → Storm Roar (taunt it back)
3. Thunder Shock (reduce boss damage output)
4. Rallying Cry (party buff)
5. Shield Wall boss's telegraphed attack
6. Swap back to Thundercry Blade → Divine Counter → Avalanche
7. Repeat
```

### Rotation (Group PvP)

```
1. Stay on Stormbreaker Spear at engagement
2. Storm Roar the enemy DPS line → force them to target you
3. Thunder Shock their highest-damage player
4. Rallying Cry your team
5. Swap to Thundercry Blade for damage once your team collapses on the taunted target
```

## How to Join Raging Tides

### Method 1: Full Membership

1. Travel to **Raging Tides Sanctum**, northeast Qinghe (across the river north of Verdant Wilds / Wildmane Ranch)
2. Collect **9 different wines** from across the game world
3. Participate in the **drinking contest** (win it)
4. Swear the military oath → you're in

### Method 2: Skill Theft (Heavenquaker Spear only)

1. Reach Kaifeng (finish Chapter 1)
2. Go to Raging Tides Sanctum
3. Equip Touch of Death Mystic Art for stealth
4. Infiltrate and step into the blue ring to steal Heavenquaker Spear
5. Stormbreaker Spear is **free at Level 3** regardless

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use both spears on the same character?**
A: Yes! Equip Heavenquaker as primary and Stormbreaker as secondary. But they're on different Arsenal paths (Bellstrike vs Stonesplit), so your Inner Way points will be split. Most players specialize in one.

**Q: Which spear is better for a new player?**
A: Stormbreaker Spear — it's free at Level 3, tanky and forgiving, and pairs naturally with the beginner-friendly Thundercry Blade.

**Q: Is Heavenquaker Spear worth joining the sect for?**
A: Yes, if you like aggressive melee DPS with big AoE. The bleed synergy with Strategic Sword is one of the highest-damage combos in the game.

**Q: Do I really have to drink every week?**
A: Yes, but "drinking" means using in-game alcohol items, not real money. Most players stockpile wine and chug one when the reminder pops up. It takes 5 seconds.

**Q: What happens if I retreat in Hero's Realm?**
A: Battle Will penalty — you lose sect standing and need to earn it back through combat. One retreat isn't catastrophic, but repeated retreats will get you demoted.

**Q: How does Stormbreaker compare to Well of Heaven (Thundercry Blade) for tanking?**
A: Both are S-tier tanks in v1.7+. Stormbreaker has better team utility (Rallying Cry, Thunder Shock debuff). Thundercry has better personal damage. Most groups want both — Stormbreaker as main tank, Thundercry as off-tank/DPS.
