## How to Unlock the Strategic Sword

### Method 1: Skill Theft (No Sect Required — Recommended)

1. Acquire the **Touch of Death Mystic Art** (from the *"The Realm Endures"* side quest)
2. Travel to **Lone Cloud Sanctum**, southwest Qinghe (near Encircling Lake / Back Mountain Boundary Stone)
3. Use stealth to navigate past patrolling guards
4. Reach the upper level observation point
5. Complete the **golden-ring Skill Theft mini-game** → Strategic Sword unlocked

### Method 2: Join Lone Cloud Sect (Level 51+)

1. Reach **Level 51+** and progress the main story
2. Travel to **Kaifeng** → east side of the central palace, near water
3. Find the **Lone Cloud senior brother** drinking tea
4. Pass a **randomized math & logic exam** (mathematics, logic puzzles, philosophy, history)
5. Swear the sect oath → full membership

## Skill Breakdown

### Inner Track Slash (Q — Martial Art)
- Dash to enemy, slash + apply Bleed
- Press again within 2s for **4 more slashes** + additional Bleed
- Your primary bleed applicator — use on cooldown

### Inner Balance Strike III (Special — Perception key)
- Dodge backward, unleash flurry
- If target has **5 Bleed stacks**: deals **massive bonus damage** and clears stacks
- This is your detonator — never use it without 5 stacks

### Second Track Slash (Charged Attack)
- Dash forward consuming Endurance
- **Untargetable & immune** during the dash
- Deals massive damage + applies Bleed
- Use as a gap-closer, escape, or burst filler

### Crosswind Blade (Dual-Weapon Swap Attack)
- Switch to Strategic Sword and immediately attack
- With Sword Horizon Inner Art: parry-cancel into Crisscrossing Swords
- Enables rapid detonation loops

## Rotation Guide

### Full Bleed Burst Rotation (Bosses)

**Phase 1 — Setup (Heavenquaker Spear):**
```
1. Dragon's Breath → apply Ignition burn DoT
2. Sober Sorrow → build combo to 10+ hits → parry-cancel → River Flow active
3. Charged Heavy Attack (1 charge) → apply 5 Soul Shaken stacks (+50% DoT damage)
```

**Phase 2 — Burst (Strategic Sword):**
```
4. TAB swap → Inner Track Slash (both stages) → 5 Bleed stacks
5. Inner Balance Strike III → DETONATE (massive burst under all buffs)
6. Crosswind Blade → parry-cancel → Crisscrossing Swords
7. Inner Balance Strike III again → second detonation
8. Second Track Slash → gap-close + damage → swap back to Spear
```

**Phase 3 — Reapply & Repeat:**
```
9. Spear: rebuild River Flow + Soul Shaken
10. Sword: reapply Bleed → detonate
```

### PvP Burst Combo

```
Dragon's Breath → Soaring Spin (gap-close) → Second Track Slash (immune dash)
→ Inner Track Slash (both) → 5 Bleed → Inner Balance Strike III
→ Crosswind Blade cancel → if target alive: Drunken Poet finisher
```

### Pro Tips

- **Never detonate before 5 stacks.** The damage difference between 4-stack and 5-stack detonation is ~60%.
- **Dragon's Breath BEFORE Drunken Poet.** Poet hits burning targets for bonus explosions — Breath first, Poet after.
- **Second Track Slash has full i-frames.** Use it to dodge boss mechanics while dealing damage. Don't waste it as a gap-closer.
- **River Flow + Soul Shaken = ~70% more detonation damage.** If your Spear setup isn't active, don't detonate.
- **Affinity Rate is your real primary stat.** Every bleed tick triggers Affinity, which triggers Hawkwing stacks, which triggers more damage. It's a cascade.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to join Lone Cloud to use Strategic Sword?**
A: No. Skill Theft at Lone Cloud Sanctum gives you the weapon and all its martial arts. You miss cosmetics and the math-exam bragging rights.

**Q: Why pair with Heavenquaker Spear specifically?**
A: River Flow (+20% HP damage) + Soul Shaken (+50% DoT damage taken) together amplify your detonation by ~70%. No other pairing matches this synergy.

**Q: What's the #1 mistake new Strategic Sword players make?**
A: Detonating at 3-4 stacks instead of waiting for 5. The burst is exponential — 4 stacks is half the damage of 5.

**Q: Is Strategic Sword good in PvP?**
A: Yes — S-tier. Second Track Slash i-frames + Bleed detonation burst can 100-to-0 squishy targets in one rotation. But you're fragile; positioning is everything.

**Q: How do I pass the Lone Cloud math exam?**
A: Questions are randomized — pure math, logic puzzles, philosophy, and history. No cheat sheet. You can retry if you fail.

**Q: Can I use Strategic Sword without Bellstrike – Umbra Inner Ways?**
A: You can, but Sword Horizon and Morale Chant are on Bellstrike – Umbra. Without them, you're at ~50% potential.
