# ⚔️ All Weapon Types & Martial Arts

A complete overview of every weapon type in Where Winds Meet, what they do, and which one fits your playstyle.

---

## Weapon Types at a Glance

| Weapon Type | Martial Arts | Role | Difficulty |
|-------------|-------------|------|------------|
| **Sword** | Nameless Sword, Strategic Sword | Melee DPS | ★★☆☆☆ |
| **Dual Blades** | Infernal Twinblades | Burst DPS | ★★★★☆ |
| **Spear** | Nameless Spear, Heavenquaker, Stormbreaker | DPS / Tank | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Mo Blade** | Thundercry Blade | Tank | ★★☆☆☆ |
| **Rope Dart** | Mortal Rope Dart | Control / DPS | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Fan** | Inkwell Fan, Panacea Fan | Ranged DPS / Healer | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Umbrella** | Vernal Umbrella, Soulshade Umbrella | Ranged DPS / Healer | ★★★★☆ |

> You can equip **two weapons at once** and swap mid-combat. Your weapon pair defines your **Arsenal Path**.

## How to Unlock Martial Arts

| Method | Description | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Join a Sect** | Swear oath → free access to sect weapon | Dedicated builds |
| **Skill Theft** | Infiltrate Sanctum → timing mini-game → steal art | Keeping freedom, no rules |
| **Qi Sheng (1st Oddity)** | Choose one weapon as your free reward | Early access to a specific weapon |
| **Story/Quests** | Some arts unlock through main/side quests | Natural progression |

## Choosing Your Weapons

| Your Style | Recommended Pairing |
|-------------|-------------------|
| "I want to tank everything" | Thundercry Blade + Stormbreaker Spear |
| "I want to heal my team" | Soulshade Umbrella + Panacea Fan |
| "I want to melt bosses fast" | Infernal Twinblades + Nameless Sword |
| "I want ranged DPS" | Vernal Umbrella + Inkwell Fan |
| "I want to kite and control" | Mortal Rope Dart + Inkwell Fan |
| "I'm brand new" | Nameless Sword + Nameless Spear |

> **Pro tip:** Don't chase the tier list. Pick weapons that feel fun. Every weapon is viable — player skill matters more than meta ranking.
