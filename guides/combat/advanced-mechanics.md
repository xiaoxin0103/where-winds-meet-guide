# 🎯 Advanced Combat Mechanics

Master these techniques to go from button-mashing to dominating every fight.

---

## The Combat Triangle

```
Block > Light Attacks > Grab > Block
   │                        │
   └────────────────────────┘
```

| Action | Beats | Loses To |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Block** | Light attacks, projectiles | Grabs, unblockables |
| **Light Attack** | Grab attempts, idle enemies | Blocks |
| **Grab/Heavy** | Blocking enemies | Light attacks (interrupted) |

---

## Perfect Dodge

Timing a dodge just before an attack lands:

| Reward | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Breath Hold** | Slow-motion window for counter-attack |
| **Cooldown Reset** | Resets Addled Mind (Twinblades) and similar skills |
| **Stamina Efficient** | Costs less than panic-dodging |

> **Practice drill:** Stand in front of a low-level enemy. Only attack after Perfect Dodge. Do this for 10 minutes until it's muscle memory.

---

## Block Countering (Divine Counter)

For **charged-attack weapons** (Thundercry Blade, Spears):

```
Block enemy attack → Free instant Stage 3 charge → Punish
```

This is your **highest DPS move** on tank/bruiser builds. Blocks also generate Fighting Spirit.

> **When to block vs. dodge:** Block physical attacks. Dodge unblockables (red glow), grabs, and AoE explosions.

---

## Animation Cancelling

Shortening recovery frames to chain actions faster:

| Cancel Type | How | Use Case |
|-------------|-----|----------|
| **Block Cancel** | Tap block mid-swing → immediately act again | Shorten Varied Combo recovery |
| **Parry Cancel** | Star Shift Mystic Art → cancel into another skill | Weapon swap safety |
| **Dodge Cancel** | Dodge out of recovery frames | Fast weapons, repositioning |

> **Practice drill:** Light attack → Block Cancel → Light attack. Repeat until smooth. Then add skills.

## Qi Break & Exhausted Windows

- **Qi (蓝色条)** is the enemy's shield bar
- Break it → enemy enters **Exhausted** state:
  - Cannot act for several seconds
  - Takes **bonus damage** from all sources
  - Vulnerable to grabs and heavy attacks

**How to break Qi faster:**
- Heavy charged attacks
- Specific Mystic Arts (Talon Strike, Yaksha Rush)
- Stormbreaker Spear → Thunder Shock (applies Vulnerable)

> **Boss rotation rule:** Save burst cooldowns for Exhausted windows. Don't waste them on shielded bosses.

## Weapon Swap Combos

Your two weapons share resources and can chain:

### Example: Tank Combo
```
Thundercry Blade: Shield up → Block counter → Stage 3 Avalanche
→ Conversion (weapon swap)
→ Stormbreaker Spear: Thunder Shock (Vulnerable) → Storm Roar (taunt)
→ Conversion (swap back)
→ Thundercry Blade: Predator's Shield → repeat
```

### Example: PvP Combo
```
Infernal Twinblades: Addled Mind (gap close) → Calamity's Greed
→ Build Karmic Flame → Flamelash (Wrathful Form)
→ Light Attack spam with CC immunity
→ Nameless Sword: Shadow Step (chase/retreat)
```

## Positioning Fundamentals

| Range | Stay Here If... |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Melee (0-3m)** | You're a tank or melee DPS. Stick to the boss's back/flank. |
| **Mid (3-8m)** | You're a healer. Close enough to heal, far enough to dodge. |
| **Far (8m+)** | You're ranged DPS. Kite and maintain distance. |

> **Universal rule:** Never stand in front of the boss unless you're the tank.

## CC & Immunity Chain

| CC Type | Counter |
|---------|---------|
| Stun | Serene Breeze (break out) |
| Knockdown | Golden Body (Fortitude = immune) |
| Grab | Dodge or Light Attack to interrupt |
| Slow | Cloud Steps (gap close anyway) |

**CC immunity stacking:** Golden Body + Flamelash = double Fortitude. Near-permanent CC immunity for Twinblade builds.

---

## Quick Reference: When to Use What

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Boss winding up a big attack | Block (tank) or Perfect Dodge (DPS) |
| Boss Qi shield breaks | Dump ALL burst cooldowns |
| Enemy player spamming blocks | Grab or Talon Strike |
| Getting CC-chained in PvP | Serene Breeze → Golden Body |
| Multiple enemies surrounding you | Sunrush Gale (AoE pull) or Ghost Bind (AoE CC) |
| Ally about to die | Light Dust After Morning Rain → Resurrection |

---

> **Bottom line:** Learn Perfect Dodge and Block Counter first. Everything else builds on these two skills.
