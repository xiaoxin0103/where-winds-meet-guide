# 🧘 Inner Arts System Explained

Inner Arts (Inner Ways) are passive skills that define your build. Choosing the right 4 makes the difference between struggling and dominating.

---

## What Are Inner Arts?

Passive abilities equipped in 4 slots that modify your combat performance. Two types:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Universal** | Work with any weapon. Flexible, always active. |
| **Martial Art-Linked** | Only active when using a specific weapon. Powerful but narrow. |

> **Common beginner mistake:** Equipping Inner Arts for weapons you don't use. Check every slot.

---

## How to Get Inner Arts

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Buy Torn Pages** | Merchants sell fragments. Game can mark vendors on your map. |
| **Solve Puzzles** | In-world puzzles unlock arts via "Comprehend Inner Way" menu. |
| **Clear Sanctums** | Stealing martial arts or looting all sanctum chests grants matching Inner Arts. |
| **Quest Chains** | Some story/side quests reward specific arts. |

---

## How to Upgrade Inner Arts

1. Each Inner Art needs its own **"Tips"** (e.g., "Sword Morph Tips") to advance tiers.
2. Buy **random Tip tomes** from the Inner Way shop (limited weekly purchases).
3. Use **Tips Exchange** to recycle unwanted Tips into Bookmarks.
4. Spend Bookmarks on **Recommendation Tomes** for your specific weapon.
5. **Inner Way Conversion** lets you swap Tip counts between two high-rarity arts (few uses per season).

## Inner Arts FAQ

**Q: Can I change Inner Arts freely?**
A: Yes, swap anytime out of combat. Experiment!

**Q: How many should I upgrade?**
A: Focus on **2-3 core arts** for your main build. Don't spread thin.

**Q: Weapon-linked vs Universal — which is better?**
A: Weapon-linked arts are usually stronger but only work with one weapon. If you swap weapons often, go universal.

**Q: When should I use Tips Exchange?**
A: When you have 10+ Tips for an art you'll never use. Convert them to something useful.
