## What is Where Winds Meet?

*Where Winds Meet* (燕云十六声) is a free-to-play open-world Wuxia RPG set in 10th-century China during the Five Dynasties era. Explore a vast world, master martial arts, join one of 11 sects, and carve your own path as a wandering swordsman.

**Released:** November 2025 (Global) | **Platforms:** PC (Steam), PS5, iOS, Android | **Developer:** Everstone Studio / NetEase
